# CDPH Phlebotomy Program Approval

## Overview

To operate a phlebotomy training program in California, you must obtain approval from the **California Department of Public Health (CDPH), Laboratory Field Services (LFS)**. This is in addition to BPPE approval.

---

## Key Information

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Regulatory Body** | CDPH - Laboratory Field Services |
| **Requirement** | Program must be CDPH-LFS approved |
| **Student Certification** | Students must obtain CDPH certificate to practice |
| **Certificate Validity** | 2 years (requires 6 CEUs for renewal) |

### Contact Information
- **Address:** Laboratory Field Services - Phlebotomy Program, 850 Marina Bay Parkway, Bldg. P, 1st Floor, Richmond, CA 94804
- **Website:** [CDPH Phlebotomy](https://www.cdph.ca.gov/Programs/OSPHLD/LFS/Pages/Phlebotomist.aspx)

## Phlebotomy Certification Levels

California has three levels of phlebotomy certification:

### Limited Phlebotomy Technician (LPT)
- **Scope:** Skin puncture (fingerstick) only
- **Training:** 20 hours classroom + 25 skin punctures
- **Market:** Limited job opportunities

### Certified Phlebotomy Technician I (CPT I)
- **Scope:** Skin puncture + venipuncture
- **Training:** 40 hours didactic + 40 hours practical
- **Practical Requirements:** 10 skin punctures + 50 venipunctures
- **Market:** Most common certification; hospital/clinic jobs

### Certified Phlebotomy Technician II (CPT II)
- **Scope:** Skin puncture + venipuncture + arterial puncture
- **Training:** Additional training beyond CPT I
- **Market:** Advanced positions; blood banks, specialized labs

**Recommendation:** Focus on CPT I certification - highest demand, most job opportunities.

## Program Requirements (CPT I)

### Didactic (Classroom) Training - 40 Hours Minimum

| Topic | Recommended Hours |
|-------|-------------------|
| Anatomy & Physiology | 8 |
| Medical Terminology | 4 |
| Infection Control & Safety | 6 |
| Blood Collection Equipment | 4 |
| Venipuncture Procedures | 8 |
| Skin Puncture Procedures | 4 |
| Specimen Handling | 4 |
| Quality Assurance | 2 |

### Practical/Clinical Training - 40 Hours Minimum

| Requirement | Minimum |
|-------------|---------|
| Supervised Clinical Hours | 40 |
| Successful Skin Punctures | 10 |
| Successful Venipunctures | 50 |

### Total Program
- **Minimum:** 80 hours (40 didactic + 40 practical)
- **Recommended:** 100-120 hours for comprehensive training
- **Duration:** 4-8 weeks typical

## Instructor Requirements

Phlebotomy program instructors must:
- Hold current CPT I or CPT II certification (or higher credential)
- Have minimum 3 years phlebotomy experience
- Demonstrate teaching competency
- Maintain current certification

### Ideal Instructor Profile
- MLT (Medical Laboratory Technician) or MLS (Medical Laboratory Scientist)
- CPT I or CPT II certification
- 5+ years clinical phlebotomy experience
- Teaching experience preferred

---

## Clinical Training Site Requirements

Clinical sites must:
- Be CLIA-certified laboratories
- Have licensed laboratory director oversight
- Provide qualified supervision (CPT I minimum)
- Allow sufficient patient volume for student draws
- Sign affiliation agreement with school

### Ideal Clinical Partners
- Hospital laboratories
- Outpatient draw stations
- Blood banks
- Urgent care centers
- Large medical group practices

---

## Student Certification Process

After completing your program, students must:

### 1. Pass National Certification Exam
Accepted exams include:
- ASCP (American Society for Clinical Pathology)
- NHA (National Healthcareer Association)
- NCCT (National Center for Competency Testing)
- AMT (American Medical Technologists)

### 2. Apply to CDPH
Submit to Laboratory Field Services:
- [ ] Completed application
- [ ] Certificate of completion from approved program
- [ ] Verification of practical training (Statement of Phlebotomy Practical Training)
- [ ] Verification of national certification
- [ ] Application fee

### 3. Receive CDPH Certificate
- Valid for 2 years
- Must display certificate number to employers
- Renewal requires 6 CEUs
