# BPPE Forms Inventory

> **Quick Reference:** Complete inventory of BPPE forms with descriptions, when to use, fees, and download links.

---

## Overview

BPPE provides standardized forms for all major transactions. Using the correct form with complete information speeds processing. All forms are available at: **www.bppe.ca.gov/forms_pubs/**

---

## Initial Approval Forms

| Form | Purpose | Fee | Processing |
|------|---------|-----|------------|
| **Application 94886** | Approval to Operate (Non-Accredited) | $5,000 | 12-24 months |
| **Application 94890** | Approval to Operate (Accredited) | $750 | 30 days (target) |
| **Application 94801.5** | Out-of-State Registration | $1,500 | 60-90 days |

### Application 94886 (Non-Accredited)
**Use When:** Starting a new school without USDE-recognized accreditation
**Requirements:** 25 sections of documentation (see [[BPPE Application Elements]])
**Attachments:** Extensive - catalog, enrollment agreement, financials, facility documents, faculty credentials

### Application 94890 (Accredited)
**Use When:** School has current USDE-recognized institutional accreditation
**Requirements:** Abbreviated application, accreditation verification letter
**Attachments:** Accreditation verification, catalog, enrollment agreement, agent designation

### Application 94801.5 (Out-of-State)
**Use When:** Out-of-state school offers distance education to California residents
**Requirements:** Registration (not full approval), proof of home state authorization
**Duration:** 5-year registration period

## Renewal Forms

| Form | Purpose | Fee | Deadline |
|------|---------|-----|----------|
| **Application 94891** | Renewal (Non-Accredited) | $3,500 | 120 days before expiration |
| **Accredited Renewal** | Renewal (Accredited) | $3,000 | 120 days before expiration |

### Renewal Application 94891
**Use When:** Renewing non-accredited approval (every 5 years)
**Requirements:** Updated documentation, current financials, outcome data
**Key Documents:**
- Current audited financial statements
- Updated catalog
- Updated School Performance Fact Sheets
- Faculty credentials (any changes)
- Program changes since last approval

## Substantive Change Forms

| Form | Change Type | Fee | Processing |
|------|-------------|-----|------------|
| **change_business.pdf** | Ownership/Control | $500-$5,000 | 60-120 days |
| **change_location.pdf** | Address Change | $500 | 30-60 days |
| **change_name.pdf** | Institution Name | $500 | 30-60 days |
| **addition_branch.pdf** | Branch Location | $3,000 | 90-180 days |
| **change_objectives.pdf** | Educational Objectives | $500 | 60-90 days |
| **change_method.pdf** | Delivery Method | $500 | 60-90 days |
| **clock_credit** | Clock to Credit Hours | $500 | 60-90 days |

### Change of Ownership/Control (change_business.pdf)
**Use When:**
- Any person acquires 25%+ ownership (change in control)
- Any person acquires 50%+ ownership (change in ownership)
- Corporate restructuring affecting control

**Requirements:**
- New owner disclosures
- Updated organizational documents
- Financial statements
- Declaration of any regulatory history

**Fee Scale:**
- Minor change in control: $500
- Major ownership change: Up to $5,000

### Change of Location (change_location.pdf)
**Use When:** Moving primary facility or branch to new address
**Requirements:**
- New lease/deed
- Floor plans
- Zoning verification
- Fire/safety compliance
- Updated facility description

### Addition of Branch (addition_branch.pdf)
**Use When:** Opening additional campus or satellite location
**Requirements:**
- All facility documentation for new location
- Staffing plan for branch
- Programs to be offered
- Enrollment projections
- Organizational chart showing branch relationship

### Change of Educational Objectives (change_objectives.pdf)
**Use When:**
- Adding new program
- Discontinuing program
- Changing program by 25%+ of hours
- Changing program objectives

**Requirements:**
- Complete program description
- Curriculum/syllabi
- Faculty qualifications
- Equipment and facilities
- Labor market justification (for new programs)

### Change of Delivery Method (change_method.pdf)
**Use When:**
- Converting from in-person to online
- Adding distance education component
- Changing LMS platform (substantive)
- Adding hybrid delivery

**Requirements:**
- Technology platform description
- Student support mechanisms
- Faculty training plan
- Assessment methods
- Student interaction procedures

## Compliance Forms

| Form | Purpose | Frequency |
|------|---------|-----------|
| **Annual Report** | Annual compliance report | Annual (Dec 1) |
| **STRF Quarterly Report** | STRF assessment reporting | Quarterly |
| **School Performance Fact Sheet** | Outcome disclosures | Annual + per student |

### Annual Report (via ar.bppe.ca.gov)
**Use When:** Required annually for all approved institutions
**Deadline:** December 1 (for preceding fiscal year)
**Contents:**
- Institutional information updates
- Enrollment data by program
- Completion rates
- Placement rates
- Financial information
- Faculty changes
- Program changes

**Submission:** Online only via ar.bppe.ca.gov

### STRF Quarterly Report
**Use When:** Required quarterly for all qualifying institutions
**Deadlines:**
- Q1: April 30
- Q2: July 31
- Q3: October 31
- Q4: January 31

**Contents:**
- Students enrolled/assessed
- Total institutional charges
- STRF assessments collected
- Remittance (when rate > $0)

## Student/Consumer Forms

| Form | Purpose | User |
|------|---------|------|
| **complaint.pdf** | Student Complaint | Students |
| **strf_application.pdf** | STRF Claim | Students |

### Student Complaint Form (complaint.pdf)
**Purpose:** File complaint against approved institution
**Types of Complaints:**
- Refund issues
- Advertising misrepresentation
- Quality of instruction
- Catalog/agreement violations

### STRF Application (strf_application.pdf)
**Purpose:** Student claim for tuition recovery
**Eligibility:** California residents who suffered economic loss due to qualifying event
**Deadline:** 4 years from qualifying event

## Tips for Faster Processing

1. **Use Current Forms:** Download directly from BPPE website - forms are periodically updated
2. **Complete All Fields:** Missing information triggers deficiency letters
3. **Attach All Required Documents:** Use checklists to verify completeness
4. **Respond Promptly:** Address deficiency letters within 30 days
5. **Follow Up:** Check application status via portal or phone after 60 days
