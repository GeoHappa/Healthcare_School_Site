# BPPE Approval Process

## Overview

The **Bureau for Private Postsecondary Education (BPPE)** is the California regulatory agency that oversees private postsecondary educational institutions under the California Private Postsecondary Education Act of 2009 (CEC §§94800-94950). Any private school offering certificate/diploma programs must obtain BPPE approval before advertising or enrolling students.

BPPE regulates over **1,300 institutions** in California with a current sunset date of **January 1, 2027**.

## The Nine Minimum Operating Standards

CEC §94885 establishes nine standards every institution must satisfy:

| # | Standard | Citation |
|---|----------|----------|
| 1 | Educational program content can achieve stated objectives | CEC §94885(a)(1) |
| 2 | Written admissions standards exist for each program | CEC §94885(a)(2) |
| 3 | Facilities, equipment, and materials are sufficient | CEC §94885(a)(3) |
| 4 | Withdrawal policy and refunds are maintained | CEC §94885(a)(4) |
| 5 | Directors, administrators, and faculty are qualified | CEC §94885(a)(5) |
| 6 | Financial soundness to fulfill student commitments | CEC §94885(a)(6) |
| 7 | Completion documents awarded upon graduation | CEC §94885(a)(7) |
| 8 | Records and transcripts maintained and available | CEC §94885(a)(8) |
| 9 | Legal compliance with all applicable laws | CEC §94885(a)(9) |

## Types of Approval

### 1. Approval to Operate - Non-Accredited (CEC §94886)
For new schools without USDE-recognized institutional accreditation.

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Form** | Application 94886 (25 sections) |
| **Fee** | $5,000 |
| **Processing** | 12-24+ months |
| **Site Visit** | Required |
| **Documentation** | Comprehensive (see [[BPPE Application Elements]]) |

**For Degree Programs:** Non-accredited institutions receive only **provisional approval** (CEC §94885.5):
- Limited to 2 degree programs maximum
- Must achieve accreditation within 5 years
- Must achieve candidacy within 2 years
- Required student disclosure about provisional status

### 2. Approval to Operate - Accredited (CEC §94890)
For schools with accreditation from USDE-recognized agencies.

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Form** | Application 94890 (abbreviated) |
| **Fee** | $750 |
| **Processing** | 30-90 days (target) |
| **Documentation** | Accreditation verification, catalog, enrollment agreement |

**Recognized Institutional Accreditors:**
- Regional: WSCUC, HLC, MSCHE, NECHE, NWCCU, SACSCOC, ACCJC
- National: ACCSC, ACCET, DEAC, COE, ABHES, TRACS

### 3. Out-of-State Registration (CEC §94801.5)
For schools headquartered outside California offering distance education.

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Form** | Application 94801.5 |
| **Fee** | $1,500 |
| **Duration** | 5-year registration |
| **Processing** | 60-90 days |

## Application Review Process (Non-Accredited)

```
Phase 1 (1-2 weeks): Receipt and Acknowledgment
├── Bureau receives application
├── Assigns tracking number
└── Issues receipt confirmation

Phase 2 (30 days): Completeness Review
├── Verifies all required documents present
├── Issues deficiency letter if incomplete
└── Determines "deemed complete" date

Phase 3 (6-18+ months): Compliance Review
├── Assigned analyst reviews all documentation
├── Verifies curriculum, faculty, financials, policies
├── Quality of Education Unit reviews programs
└── Multiple deficiency rounds may occur

Phase 4 (As Needed): Deficiency Responses
├── 30 days to respond to each deficiency letter
├── Address each item specifically
└── Provide supporting documentation

Phase 5 (If Applicable): Visiting Committee
├── Mandatory for provisional approval (degree programs)
├── Discretionary for certificate/diploma programs
├── 30 days notice of committee membership
├── 14 days to challenge members

Phase 6: Site Visit
├── BPPE schedules inspection
├── Verifies facility, equipment, records
├── May interview staff
└── Exit conference with preliminary findings

Phase 7: Decision
├── Manager reviews analyst recommendations
├── Approval, denial, or conditional authorization
└── Conditional authorization: Up to 6 months for minor issues
```

**Current Processing Status (January 2026):** Bureau reviewing applications deemed complete in September 2024 (approximately 16-month backlog).

## Application Requirements Summary

### Business/Corporate Documents
- [ ] Articles of Incorporation/Organization
- [ ] Bylaws or Operating Agreement
- [ ] Fictitious Business Name Statement (if DBA)
- [ ] Organizational Chart (governance + administrative)
- [ ] Ownership disclosure (all persons with 25%+ ownership or substantial control)
- [ ] CEO, COO, CAO resumes
- [ ] Job descriptions for all positions

### Financial Documents
- [ ] **Audited financial statements** (GAAP compliant)
  - Prepared by independent CPA
  - **No more than 120 days old** at submission
  - Balance sheet and income statement
- [ ] Proof of sufficient resources to fulfill commitments

### Facility Documents
- [ ] Lease agreement or deed
- [ ] Building diagrams/floor plans (classrooms, labs identified)
- [ ] Equipment specifications with ownership status
- [ ] Permits, certifications, inspections

### Program Documents
- [ ] Curriculum for each program with syllabi
- [ ] 3-year enrollment projections with basis
- [ ] Learning outcomes/competencies
- [ ] Licensing agency approval (if licensure programs)
- [ ] Accreditation plan (if degree programs)

### Student Protection Documents
- [ ] **Enrollment Agreement** with completed checklist (23 elements)
- [ ] **School Catalog** with completed checklist (42+ elements)
- [ ] **School Performance Fact Sheet** for each program
- [ ] Graduation/completion documents (diploma/certificate samples)

### Administrative Documents
- [ ] Agent for Service of Process designation (separate address required)
- [ ] Mission and objectives statement
- [ ] Self-monitoring/compliance procedures
- [ ] Recordkeeping procedures
- [ ] Job placement assistance description (if offered)
- [ ] Library/learning resources description
- [ ] All advertising materials

**For complete section-by-section requirements, see [[BPPE Application Elements]].**

## Enrollment Agreement Requirements (23 Elements)

Per CEC §94911 and 5 CCR §71800, enrollment agreements must include:

### Financial Terms
- **Itemized charges** for 12 categories (tuition, fees, equipment, textbooks, STRF, etc.)
- **UNDERLINED CAPITAL LETTERS on signature page:**
  - Total charges for current enrollment period
  - Estimated total charges for entire program
  - Total charges student is obligated to pay upon enrollment

### Required Disclosures
- STUDENT'S RIGHT TO CANCEL (7-day/first session)
- STRF disclosure language (verbatim per 5 CCR §76215)
- Transfer of credits disclosure (verbatim language)
- Loan repayment consequences
- Bureau contact information
- "Legally binding contract" statement

### Student Acknowledgments
- Student initial line for catalog/SPFS receipt
- Student and authorized employee signatures

---

## Catalog Requirements (42+ Elements)

Per CEC §94909 and 5 CCR §71810, catalogs must include:

### Institutional Information
- Institution name, address, phone, website
- BPPE approval status and Bureau contact for complaints
- Accreditation status (or statement if unaccredited)

### Educational Information
- Mission statement and program objectives
- Program descriptions with course outlines
- Credit/clock hour requirements
- Admissions requirements and transfer policies
- Transferability disclosure (verbatim language)

### Policies
- Attendance, grading, SAP, probation, dismissal
- Leave of absence, re-admission
- Grievance/complaint procedures
- Non-discrimination statement

### Financial Information
- Total charges schedule
- Payment terms
- Cancellation and refund policies
- **STRF disclosure** (verbatim language)
- Financial aid information (if applicable)

### Faculty and Facilities
- Faculty names and qualifications
- Facility and equipment descriptions
- Library resources

### Occupational Information
- SOC codes for each program
- License exam passage rates (if licensure program)
- Placement assistance description (if offered)

## School Performance Fact Sheet (SPFS)

Required for each educational program per CEC §94910.

### Format Requirements
- Minimum 12-point readable font
- 1.15 line spacing
- Bold 14-point titles

### Required Data (for 2 most recent years)
- **Completion rates:** Students who began vs. on-time graduates
- **Placement rates:** Graduates employed in field (6 months post-graduation)
- **License exam passage rates:** First-time pass rates (if licensure program)
- **Salary information:** Annual wages for employed graduates (if salary claims made)
- **Student loan data:** Cohort default rate, % receiving federal loans

### Student Acknowledgment
- Student must initial and date each section before enrollment
- Student and institution representative signatures required

## Ongoing Compliance

### Annual Requirements
- [ ] File Annual Report via ar.bppe.ca.gov (due **December 1**)
- [ ] Update School Performance Fact Sheets
- [ ] Pay annual fees
- [ ] Submit quarterly STRF reports (even when $0)
- [ ] Maintain student records
- [ ] Post annual report on website

### Substantive Changes (Require Prior Approval)
- New program offerings
- Change in ownership (50%+) or control (25%+)
- Change in location
- Change in instructional method
- Name change
- Branch additions

See [[BPPE Forms Inventory]] for change application forms.

### Record Retention
| Record Type | Retention Period |
|-------------|------------------|
| Student transcripts | **Permanently** |
| Enrollment agreements | 5 years after completion/withdrawal |
| Financial records | 5 years |
| Faculty records | 5 years |
| Advertising | 5 years |

### Compliance Inspections
- Announced: Every 5 years minimum
- Unannounced: May occur based on complaints, late reports, or tips
- See [[BPPE Enforcement Guide]] for enforcement details

## Common Application Mistakes

| Issue | Prevention |
|-------|------------|
| **Incomplete ownership disclosure** | List ALL persons with 25%+ ownership or substantial control |
| **Financial statements too old** | Submit within 120 days of preparation date |
| **Missing catalog elements** | Use BPPE Catalog Checklist, verify all 42+ elements |
| **Enrollment agreement deficiencies** | Use BPPE Agreement Checklist, verify all 23 elements |
| **Missing STRF disclosure** | Use verbatim language from 5 CCR §76215 |
| **Vague curriculum** | Include specific learning objectives, assessments, competencies |
| **Unqualified faculty** | Document 3+ years experience or relevant credentials |
| **Agent for Service at school address** | Agent address must be different from institution |
| **Missing signature page elements** | Total charges in UNDERLINED CAPITAL LETTERS |

## Practical Guidance

### Before Applying
1. **Attend BPPE workshop** - Free, essential orientation
2. **Review CEC §§94800-94950** and 5 CCR Division 7.5
3. **Consult education attorney** for application review
4. **Use official checklists** - Catalog and Enrollment Agreement checklists from BPPE
5. **Plan for extended timelines** - Budget 12-24+ months for non-accredited

### During Review
1. **Respond within 30 days** to deficiency letters
2. **Address each item specifically** with documentation
3. **Keep copies of all correspondence**
4. **Check status** via portal after 60 days

### After Approval
1. **Display approval prominently** at primary location
2. **Establish compliance infrastructure** - calendars, records systems
3. **Begin STRF reporting** immediately (quarterly)
4. **Calendar all deadlines** - Annual report (Dec 1), renewal (6 months before expiration)
