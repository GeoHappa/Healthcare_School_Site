## Executive Summary

[SCHOOL NAME] is a proposed private postsecondary vocational school offering healthcare training programs in Folsom, California. The school will provide certificate programs in Medical Assistant, Phlebotomy Technician, Medical Coding, and EKG Technician to prepare students for entry-level healthcare careers.

### The Opportunity

Folsom represents an exceptional market opportunity:
- **Population:** 84,000 with median household income of $139,263
- **Gap in market:** Zero MA, Phlebotomy, or Medical Coding programs within city limits
- **Healthcare demand:** 270+ MA job postings monthly in Sacramento region with 10% unmet demand
- **Target demographics:** 56.5% working-age population, highly educated (58.5% bachelor's+)

### Financial Highlights

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Total Investment Required | $230,000 - $350,000 |
| Year 1 Projected Revenue | $705,000 |
| Year 1 Net Income | $185,000 |
| Year 3 Projected Revenue | $1.4 million |
| Break-Even | Year 1 |
| 3-Year ROI | 472% |

### Key Success Factors

1. Secure BPPE approval before marketing
2. Establish 3-5 clinical partnerships for externships
3. Hire qualified Program Director by Month 3
4. Achieve ABHES accreditation for CMA eligibility (Year 2-3)
5. Maintain 70%+ job placement rate

### Healthcare Industry Demand

**California Nursing/Healthcare Shortage:**
- 25,168 FTE RN shortage in 2024
- Sacramento designated as shortage area
- Healthcare represents 20% of regional payroll

**Medical Assistant Demand:**
- 270 job postings per month in Sacramento

## Programs & Services

*Detailed curricula: [[../11 - Templates & Checklists/BPPE Documents/Programs/Programs Overview]]*

### Program Portfolio

| Program | Duration | Tuition | Certification |
|---------|----------|---------|---------------|
| Medical Assistant | 800 hours (6-9 mo) | $12,500 | CMA, RMA, CCMA |
| Phlebotomy Technician | 80 hours (4 wks) | $1,800 | CA CPT I |
| Medical Coding | 400 hours (5-6 mo) | $8,500 | CPC, CCS |
| EKG Technician | 50 hours (2 wks) | $1,200 | CET |

### Medical Assistant Program (Flagship)

**Revenue Driver:** 60% of projected revenue

| Component | Hours |
|-----------|-------|
| Didactic/Classroom | 520 |
| Lab/Skills Practice | 80 |
| Clinical Externship | 200 |
| **Total** | **800** |

**Key Topics:**
- Medical terminology and anatomy
- Clinical procedures (vital signs, injections, phlebotomy, EKG)
- Administrative skills (EHR, billing, scheduling)
- Externship at local healthcare facility

### Phlebotomy Technician Program

**Quick Completion:** 4 weeks, high throughput

- CDPH-approved curriculum (40 hrs didactic + 40 hrs clinical)
- 50 successful venipunctures required
- Prepares for California CPT I certification

### Medical Coding Specialist Program

**Remote-Work Potential:** Appeals to tech-savvy population

- ICD-10-CM, ICD-10-PCS, CPT, HCPCS Level II
- Prepares for CPC (AAPC) or CCS (AHIMA) certification
- No externship required

### EKG Technician Program

**Add-On Credential:** Short program, enhances employability

- Cardiac anatomy and rhythm interpretation
- 12-lead EKG acquisition
- Prepares for CET certification

## Risk Analysis

### Key Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| BPPE approval delay | Medium | High | Submit complete application, respond quickly to requests |
| Low initial enrollment | Medium | High | Strong marketing, pre-enrollment campaign |
| Clinical site shortage | Medium | High | Begin outreach early, have backup sites |
| Instructor turnover | Low | Medium | Competitive pay, professional development |
| Competition entry | Low | Medium | Establish market presence quickly |
| Economic downturn | Low | High | Maintain reserve, flexible cost structure |

### Contingency Plans

**If Enrollment Falls Short:**
- Increase marketing spend
- Offer promotional pricing
- Add evening/weekend cohorts
- Pursue corporate training contracts

**If Cash Flow Tightens:**
- Delay non-essential purchases
- Negotiate vendor terms
- Draw on line of credit
- Reduce part-time staff hours
