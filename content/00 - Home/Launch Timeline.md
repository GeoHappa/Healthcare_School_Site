# Healthcare Training Center Launch Timeline

---

## Overview

This timeline outlines the complete process from initial planning to first student enrollment. The process typically takes **12-18 months** depending on BPPE processing times and facility/staffing readiness.

**Target:** First cohort enrollment

## Phase 4: Approval Period
### Months 5-12

**Objective:** Respond to BPPE inquiries and complete facility build-out

---

### Month 5-8: BPPE Review Period

#### What to Expect
- BPPE reviews application (60-90 days initial review)
- May receive deficiency letter requesting additional information
- Must respond within timeframe specified
- Multiple rounds of questions possible

#### During This Time
- [ ] Monitor email/mail for BPPE correspondence
- [ ] Respond promptly to any requests (within 30 days)
- [ ] Keep all documents updated
- [ ] Do NOT advertise or enroll students yet

#### Facility Build-Out (Can proceed during review)
- [ ] Complete tenant improvements
- [ ] Install classroom furniture
- [ ] Set up lab areas
- [ ] Install equipment
- [ ] Obtain fire clearance
- [ ] Obtain occupancy permit

---

## Critical Path Items

These items are on the critical path and will delay launch if not completed:

| Item | Why Critical | Target Completion |
|------|--------------|-------------------|
| Business entity formation | Required for everything | Month 1 |
| Facility lease signed | Required for BPPE | Month 3 |
| Program Director hired | Required for BPPE | Month 3 |
| Clinical agreements signed | Required for BPPE | Month 4 |
| BPPE application submitted | Starts approval clock | Month 5 |
| BPPE approval received | Cannot enroll without it | Month 12 |
| CDPH approval (phlebotomy) | Cannot offer program without it | Month 10 |

---

## Risk Factors & Mitigation

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|
| BPPE delays | Extends timeline 2-6 months | Submit complete application, respond quickly |
| Facility issues | Delays build-out | Start search early, have backup options |
| Can't find qualified PD | Can't apply | Begin recruiting Month 1 |
| Clinical sites decline | Application incomplete | Start outreach early, contact many sites |
| Funding shortfall | Can't complete build-out | Secure funding before starting |
| CDPH delays | Can't offer phlebotomy | Apply early, have MA/EKG ready first |
