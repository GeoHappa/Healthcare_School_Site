## Critical Success Factors

1. **Funding** - Secure $300K minimum before Phase 2
2. **Program Director** - Hire qualified PD by Month 3
3. **Clinical Partnerships** - Secure 3-5 externship sites before enrollment
4. **BPPE Approval** - Allow **12-24 months** for application processing (current backlog)
5. **Instructor Quality** - Hire certified instructors with clinical networks
6. **Job Placement** - Track and publish outcomes from Day 1
7. **Cash Flow** - Maintain 2+ months operating reserve

> **BPPE Alert:** As of January 2026, BPPE processing time for non-accredited applications is 12-24+ months. Plan timeline accordingly. See [[../02 - Regulatory & Licensing/BPPE Approval Process]] for current status.
