### Lease Negotiation Points

| Term | Target | Notes |
|------|--------|-------|
| Length | 3-5 years | Stability for BPPE |
| Monthly rent | Market rate | Folsom: $1.50-2.50/sq ft |
| Deposit | 1-2 months | Negotiate down if possible |
| Tenant improvements | Landlord contribution | $10-20/sq ft common |
| Build-out period | 2-3 months free | To complete improvements |
| Signage rights | Exterior signage | Important for visibility |
| HVAC | Landlord maintains | Or allowance |
| Parking | Included | Verify count |
| Option to renew | Yes | Protects investment |

## Red Flags / Issues to Address

| Issue | Risk Level | Mitigation |
|-------|------------|------------|
| Can't find qualified PD | High | Start search early, use recruiters |
| Facility zoning issues | High | Verify before signing lease |
| Clinical sites declining | Medium | Contact many sites, start early |
| Funding delays | High | Have backup funding sources |
| Attorney delays | Medium | Engage attorney early |
