## Month 1: Business Formation

### Week 1-2: Legal Structure

#### Business Entity Formation
| Task | Status | Due | Notes |
|------|--------|-----|-------|
| Decide business structure (LLC vs Corp) | [ ] | Week 1 | LLC simpler; Corp for investors |
| Choose business name | [ ] | Week 1 | Check availability with CA SOS |
| Reserve business name | [ ] | Week 1 | Optional but recommended |
| Draft operating agreement/bylaws | [ ] | Week 1 | Attorney recommended |
| File with CA Secretary of State | [ ] | Week 2 | Online or mail |
| Receive confirmation/entity number | [ ] | Week 2 | 2-5 business days online |
| Apply for EIN (IRS) | [ ] | Week 2 | Free, instant online |
| File Statement of Information | [ ] | Week 2 | Required within 90 days |

**Resources:**
- CA Secretary of State: bizfile.sos.ca.gov
- IRS EIN: irs.gov/businesses/small-businesses-self-employed/apply-for-an-employer-identification-number-ein-online

## Key Decisions to Make

### Business Structure
| Option | Pros | Cons |
|--------|------|------|
| LLC | Simpler, flexible | Self-employment tax |
| S-Corp | Tax advantages | More paperwork |
| C-Corp | Best for investors | Double taxation possible |

**Recommendation:** LLC initially, can convert later if needed

### School Name Considerations
- Avoid names too similar to existing schools
- Include location (Folsom, Sacramento) for SEO
- Professional and credible
- Easy to spell and remember
- Check BPPE database for conflicts
