## Choosing the Right Program

### Medical Assistant
**Best for students who want to:**
- Work directly with patients
- Perform both clinical and administrative tasks
- Have variety in daily work
- Build toward nursing or other clinical careers

### Phlebotomy Technician
**Best for students who want to:**
- Enter healthcare quickly
- Specialize in blood collection
- Work in laboratories
- Have flexible scheduling options

### Medical Coding Specialist
**Best for students who want to:**
- Work behind the scenes
- Use analytical skills
- Have remote work opportunities
- Focus on healthcare business side

### EKG Technician
**Best for students who want to:**
- Add skills to existing credentials
- Specialize in cardiac care
- Quick entry-level credential
- Combine with MA or Phlebotomy
