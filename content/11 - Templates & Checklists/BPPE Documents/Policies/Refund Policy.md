## Refund for Cancellation Before Classes Begin

### Refund Amount

If you cancel BEFORE the start of classes:
- Full refund of all charges paid
- MINUS non-refundable registration fee (maximum $250)

### Refund Timeline

Refund will be paid within **45 days** of cancellation.

---

## Refund for Withdrawal After Classes Begin

### Pro-Rata Refund Calculation

If you withdraw AFTER instruction has begun, you are entitled to a **pro-rata refund** calculated as follows:

```
Refund = (Total Hours Paid - Hours Attended) ÷ Total Hours Paid × Tuition
```

### Refund Formula

**Step 1:** Calculate percentage of program completed
```
Percentage Completed = Hours Attended ÷ Total Program Hours
```

**Step 2:** Calculate refund percentage
```
Refund Percentage = 100% - Percentage Completed
```

**Step 3:** Calculate refund amount
```
Refund = Tuition Paid × Refund Percentage
```

## Determination of Withdrawal Date

The date of withdrawal is determined as:

1. **Student notification** - The date the student notifies the school of intent to withdraw, OR

2. **Last date of attendance** - If student stops attending without notification, the last documented date of attendance, OR

3. **Leave of Absence expiration** - If student fails to return from approved leave of absence, the scheduled return date, OR

4. **Dismissal date** - The date the school dismisses the student

If a student does not attend for 14 consecutive calendar days without notification, the student is considered withdrawn as of the last date of attendance.

---

## Refund Payment

### Timeline

All refunds will be paid within **45 days** of:
- The date of cancellation, OR
- The date the school determines the student has withdrawn

### Payment Method

Refunds will be made:
- To the original payment source when possible
- By check if original payment method unavailable
- To third-party payers (loans, agencies) as required by agreements

### Refund Calculation Example

**Student A:**
- Program: Medical Assistant (800 hours)
- Tuition Paid: $15,000
- Hours Attended: 200 hours
- Percentage Completed: 200 ÷ 800 = 25%
- Refund Percentage: 75%
- Refund Amount: $15,000 × 75% = **$11,250**

## Special Circumstances

### Rejected Applicants

If an applicant is rejected for enrollment or if a canceled course cannot be rescheduled:
- Full refund of all monies paid
- No registration fee retained
- Refund within 45 days

### Course Cancellation by School

If the school cancels a program before completion:
- Full refund of unearned tuition
- Pro-rata calculation based on services not delivered
- Assistance with teach-out options when available

### Military Withdrawal

Students called to active military duty:
- Pro-rata refund as calculated above
- No penalty for military withdrawal
- May resume program upon return (within 5 years)

---

## Third-Party Payments

### Financing Companies

If tuition was paid through financing:
- Refund paid to financing company
- Student remains responsible for any balance owed to lender

### Workforce Agencies (WIOA, EDD)

If tuition was paid by workforce agency:
- Refund paid to agency
- Per terms of agency agreement

### Employer Payments

If tuition was paid by employer:
- Refund paid to employer
- Per terms of employer agreement

---

## Books and Equipment

### Return Policy

- Issued materials may be returned for credit toward refund
- Must be in new, unused condition
- Return within 10 days of withdrawal

### Non-Returnable Items

The following cannot be returned:
- Used or damaged books
- Opened supplies
- Personalized items
- Items with student name/writing
