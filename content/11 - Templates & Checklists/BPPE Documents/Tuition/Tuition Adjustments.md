# Tuition Adjustments

---

## Overview

This page explains situations where tuition may be adjusted, including transfer credit reductions, leave of absence impacts, and other circumstances that affect tuition charges.

---

## Types of Adjustments

### Transfer Credit Adjustments

When credits are accepted from another institution:
- Tuition may be reduced proportionally
- Reduction based on hours credited
- See [[Transfer Credit Policy]] for eligibility

**Calculation:**
```
Credit Hours Transferred / Total Program Hours Ã— Tuition = Reduction
```

### Prior Learning Assessment Credit

When credit is awarded for prior experience:
- Tuition may be reduced
- Based on competencies demonstrated
- Assessment fee may apply

## Transfer Credit Tuition Reduction

### How It Works

1. Submit [[Transfer Credit Request Form|transfer credit request]]
2. Provide official transcripts
3. Credits evaluated and approved
4. Tuition adjusted accordingly
5. Reflected in enrollment agreement

### Example Calculation

| Item | Value |
|------|-------|
| Program total hours | 800 |
| Program tuition | $15,000 |
| Transfer credits approved | 80 hours |
| Per-hour rate | $18.75 |
| Tuition reduction | $1,500 |
| Adjusted tuition | $13,500 |

### Limitations

- Maximum transferable: 25% of program hours
- Some courses may not be eligible
- Must meet recency requirements
- Subject to Director approval

## Leave of Absence Impact

### During Leave

- No additional tuition charged
- Existing balance remains
- Payment arrangements may be modified

### After Leave

| Situation | Impact |
|-----------|--------|
| Return to same cohort | No additional tuition |
| Return to later cohort | Tuition at current rates may apply |
| Do not return | [[Refund Policy]] applies |

See [[Leave of Absence]] for complete policy.

## Program Change Adjustments

### Changing Programs

If approved to change programs:

| Scenario | Adjustment |
|----------|------------|
| Similar program (lower cost) | Refund or credit difference |
| Similar program (higher cost) | Pay difference |
| Different program | New enrollment, refund per policy |

### Process

1. Request program change in writing
2. Meet with advisor
3. Financial implications reviewed
4. If approved, new agreement signed
5. Adjustments applied

---

## Withdrawal and Refunds

### Refund Calculations

When withdrawing from program:
- Refund based on completion percentage
- Federal/state regulations apply
- See [[Refund Policy]] for complete details

### Pro-Rata Refund Period

| Completion | Refund |
|------------|--------|
| 0-10% | 90% tuition refund |
| 10-25% | 75% tuition refund |
| 25-50% | 50% tuition refund |
| 50%+ | No refund |

*Actual percentages per enrollment agreement*

---

## Scholarship and Grant Adjustments

### When Scholarships Apply

- Reduce out-of-pocket tuition
- Applied to tuition balance
- Do not affect total program cost

### Impact of Withdrawal

If student withdraws:
- Scholarship may be forfeited
- May need to repay portion
- Per scholarship terms

## Payment Plan Modifications

### When Plans May Be Modified

- Change in financial circumstances
- Employment changes
- Leave of absence
- Tuition adjustments

### Process

1. Contact Business Office
2. Explain circumstances
3. Provide documentation if requested
4. New plan developed
5. Updated agreement signed

See [[Payment Plan Agreement]] for terms.

---

## Disputes and Appeals

### If You Disagree with Adjustment

1. Request written explanation
2. Review enrollment agreement
3. Meet with Business Office
4. Submit written appeal if needed
5. Director review for final decision

### Documentation

Keep copies of:
- Enrollment agreement
- Payment receipts
- Correspondence
- Adjustment notifications

---

## No Adjustments For

The following do not result in tuition adjustments:
- Attending different schedule
- Retaking failed courses
- Extended completion time (within max)
- Switching instructors
- Personal preferences
