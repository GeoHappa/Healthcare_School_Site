## Types of VA Benefits

### GI Bill Programs

| Benefit | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Post-9/11 GI Bill (Chapter 33) | For those who served after 9/10/2001 |
| Montgomery GI Bill (Chapter 30) | For active duty service members |
| Montgomery GI Bill-SR (Chapter 1606) | For Selected Reserve members |
| Vocational Rehabilitation (Chapter 31) | For veterans with service-connected disability |
| Survivors' and Dependents' (Chapter 35) | For family members of disabled/deceased veterans |
| VRRAP | Veteran Rapid Retraining Assistance Program |

### Other Military Benefits

- MyCAA (Military Spouse Career Advancement)
- Tuition Assistance (active duty)
- State veteran education benefits

## Eligibility

### GI Bill Eligibility

Determined by VA based on:
- Length and type of service
- Discharge status (honorable, general)
- Service era
- Remaining benefit entitlement

### How to Check Eligibility

1. Visit www.va.gov/education/eligibility
2. Call VA at 1-888-442-4551
3. Submit VA Form 22-1990 (first use) or 22-1995 (transfer)

## Payment Process

### How VA Benefits Are Paid

| Benefit Type | Payment To |
|--------------|------------|
| Post-9/11 Tuition | Paid directly to school |
| Post-9/11 Housing | Paid to student |
| Post-9/11 Books | Paid to student |

### Timing

- VA payments may take 4-6 weeks initially
- Ongoing payments made monthly
- School may require payment arrangements pending VA payment

## Vocational Rehabilitation (Chapter 31)

### What It Covers

For veterans with service-connected disabilities:
- Tuition and fees
- Books and supplies
- Monthly subsistence allowance
- Employment services

### Process

1. Apply through VA
2. Work with VR&E counselor
3. Develop rehabilitation plan
4. Obtain approval for specific program
5. Counselor coordinates with school

**Contact VR&E:**
- VA: 1-800-827-1000
- Local VA Regional Office

---

## Survivors' and Dependents' (DEA)

### Eligibility

- Spouse or child of veteran who died in service
- Spouse or child of veteran with permanent, total service-connected disability
- Other qualifying circumstances

### Benefits

- Monthly education benefit
- Covers tuition and living expenses
- Up to 45 months of benefits
