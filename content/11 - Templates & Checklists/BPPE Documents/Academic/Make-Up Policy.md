# Make-Up Policy

---

## Purpose

This policy establishes procedures for students to make up missed coursework, including classroom hours, examinations, and assignments due to excused absences.

---

## Eligibility for Make-Up Work

### Excused Absences

Make-up opportunities are available for absences due to:
- Documented illness (self or dependent)
- Medical appointments (when class time is unavoidable)
- Death in immediate family
- Jury duty or court appearance
- Military obligations
- School-approved activities
- Other emergencies approved by instructor

### Documentation Required

| Absence Type | Documentation |
|--------------|---------------|
| Illness | Doctor's note or urgent care receipt |
| Medical appointment | Appointment verification |
| Family death | Obituary or funeral program |
| Jury duty | Court documentation |
| Court appearance | Subpoena or court order |
| Military | Military orders |

---

## Make-Up Procedures

### Classroom/Lab Hours

**Lecture Hours:**
- Review recorded content (if available)
- Complete assigned readings
- Submit reflection or summary assignment
- Meet with instructor during office hours

**Lab Hours:**
- Schedule make-up lab session with instructor
- Must be completed within 2 weeks of absence
- Same competencies must be demonstrated
- Limited lab sessions available per term

### Examinations

**Missed Exam Procedure:**
1. Notify instructor before exam if possible
2. Provide documentation within 48 hours of return
3. Schedule make-up exam within 1 week
4. Make-up exam may differ from original

**Make-Up Exam Rules:**
- Must be scheduled with instructor
- Taken in proctored environment
- Same time limit as original
- May be different version of exam

### Assignments

**Missed Assignment Procedure:**
1. Contact instructor immediately upon return
2. Request extension in writing
3. Submit within agreed timeframe
4. Late penalty may be reduced/waived for excused absence

---

## Time Limits

| Work Type | Make-Up Deadline |
|-----------|------------------|
| Daily assignments | 3 school days after return |
| Lab hours | 2 weeks after absence |
| Quizzes | 1 week after return |
| Exams | 1 week after return |
| Projects | Per instructor discretion |

*Extensions beyond these limits require Director approval.*

---

## Unexcused Absences

For unexcused absences:
- Make-up work is NOT guaranteed
- Instructor may offer make-up at discretion
- Late penalties apply
- Zero grade may be assigned for missed exams
- Missed lab hours count against attendance

---

## Limits on Make-Up Work

### Maximum Make-Up Hours

| Program | Max Make-Up Hours |
|---------|-------------------|
| Medical Assistant | 80 hours (10% of program) |
| Phlebotomy | 9 hours (10% of program) |
| Medical Coding | 46 hours (10% of program) |
| EKG | 5 hours (10% of program) |

*Students exceeding make-up limits may need to extend enrollment or repeat the program.*

### Exam Retakes
- Maximum 2 make-up exams per module
- Third failure may result in course failure
- See [[Retake Policy]] for exam retake procedures

## Student Responsibilities

1. **Notify promptly** - Contact instructor before or immediately after absence
2. **Provide documentation** - Submit within 48 hours of return
3. **Request make-up** - Do not assume automatic accommodation
4. **Meet deadlines** - Complete work within specified timeframes
5. **Communicate** - Keep instructor informed of any issues

---

## Instructor Responsibilities

1. Provide clear make-up instructions
2. Offer reasonable accommodation for excused absences
3. Maintain fairness across all students
4. Document all make-up agreements
5. Report excessive absences to administration

---

## Impact on Grades

### Excused Absence (with make-up)
- No grade penalty if completed on time
- Full credit available for work

### Excused Absence (no make-up available)
- Instructor discretion on grade impact
- May adjust grade calculation

### Unexcused Absence
- Zero credit for missed work
- Standard late penalties apply
- Impacts overall grade

---

## Special Circumstances

### Extended Illness
Students missing more than 5 consecutive days should:
- Contact Student Services
- Discuss Leave of Absence option
- Develop re-entry plan with instructor
- See [[Leave of Absence]]

### Clinical/Externship Hours
- Clinical hours generally cannot be made up
- May require extended enrollment period
- See [[Externship Requirements]]

---

## Appeals

Students may appeal make-up decisions by:
1. Submitting written appeal to Program Director
2. Including documentation and circumstances
3. Requesting meeting if needed
4. Receiving decision within 5 business days
