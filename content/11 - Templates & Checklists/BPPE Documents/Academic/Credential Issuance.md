## Requirements for Credential Issuance

### Academic Requirements

To receive your credential, you must:

| Requirement | Standard |
|-------------|----------|
| Minimum GPA | 2.0 cumulative |
| Course completion | All required courses passed (70%+) |
| Skills competencies | All required skills passed |
| Attendance | Minimum 80% overall |
| Externship/Clinical | All hours completed (if applicable) |
| Final exam | Passed with 70%+ |

### Administrative Requirements

You must also:

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Financial balance | Account paid in full or payment plan current |
| Return materials | All school property returned |
| Exit interview | Complete exit counseling (if required) |
| Employment survey | Complete graduate survey |

## Graduation Process

### Step 1: Completion Verification

Once you complete your final requirements:
- Instructor verifies all coursework complete
- Clinical coordinator confirms externship hours (if applicable)
- Registrar reviews academic record
- Business office confirms financial status

### Step 2: Credential Preparation

After verification:
- Credential printed with your name as registered
- Final transcript prepared
- Date of completion recorded
- Records updated

### Step 3: Credential Pickup or Mailing

**Pickup:**
- Available approximately [2] weeks after completion
- Contact Student Services to schedule
- Bring photo ID

**Mailing:**
- Request mailing on exit paperwork
- Sent to address on file
- Allow 3-4 weeks for delivery
- Sent via [regular mail/certified mail]

## Name on Credential

### Legal Name
Your credential will display your legal name as recorded in your student file.

### Name Changes
If you need a name change:
1. Submit legal documentation (court order, marriage certificate)
2. Request update before completion if possible
3. Updated credential may take additional time

### Corrections
If your name is spelled incorrectly:
- Notify Registrar immediately
- Provide correct documentation
- Replacement issued at no charge if school error

## Holds on Credentials

### Your credential may be held if:

| Reason | Resolution |
|--------|------------|
| Outstanding balance | Pay balance or arrange payment plan |
| Library materials owed | Return all materials |
| Equipment not returned | Return equipment |
| Exit paperwork incomplete | Complete required forms |
| Unresolved academic issues | Meet with Program Director |

### Resolving Holds
1. Contact Student Services or Business Office
2. Determine specific requirements
3. Complete necessary actions
4. Request hold removal

## Replacement Credentials

### If Lost or Damaged

| Situation | Process |
|-----------|---------|
| Lost | Submit request form + fee |
| Damaged | Return damaged credential + fee |
| Destroyed | Submit notarized statement + fee |

### Replacement Timeline
- Standard processing: 2-3 weeks
- Rush processing available: 5-7 business days (additional fee)

### Historical Credentials
- Records maintained per retention policy
- Older credentials may take longer to replace
- Contact Registrar for historical requests

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: When will I receive my credential?**
A: Typically 2-3 weeks after completing all requirements.

**Q: Can I get my credential early?**
A: Credentials are issued after verification. Rush processing may be available.

**Q: What if I have a balance owed?**
A: Complete credentials are held until accounts are settled. Contact Business Office.

**Q: Can I use my credential before receiving the physical document?**
A: Completion letters may be available for urgent employment needs.

**Q: What if my credential has an error?**
A: Contact Registrar immediately for correction at no charge.
