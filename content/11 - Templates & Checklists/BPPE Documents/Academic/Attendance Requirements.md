# Attendance Requirements

---

## Minimum Standards

| Component | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| Overall Program | 80% minimum attendance |
| Individual Courses | 80% minimum attendance |
| Externship/Clinical | 100% of assigned hours |

---

## Why Attendance Matters

Regular attendance is critical because:

1. **Skill Development** - Healthcare skills require consistent practice
2. **Sequential Learning** - Each lesson builds on previous content
3. **Professional Habits** - Employers expect reliability
4. **Certification Prep** - Adequate seat time for exam readiness
5. **Clinical Eligibility** - Must complete theory before externship

---

## Attendance Tracking

### Daily Sign-In/Sign-Out
- Sign attendance log at start of class
- Sign out at end of class
- Instructor verifies attendance daily
- Electronic backup maintained

### Calculating Attendance Rate

```
Attendance Rate = (Hours Attended รท Hours Scheduled) ร 100
```

**Example:**
- Scheduled: 100 hours
- Attended: 85 hours
- Rate: 85 รท 100 = 85%

## Attendance Status Levels

| Rate | Status | Action |
|------|--------|--------|
| 90-100% | Excellent | None required |
| 85-89% | Good | Monitor |
| 80-84% | Satisfactory | Verbal counseling |
| 75-79% | Warning | Written warning, improvement plan |
| 70-74% | Probation | Probation, weekly monitoring |
| Below 70% | Critical | Subject to dismissal |

---

## Impact of Poor Attendance

### Academic Consequences
- May miss critical content
- Difficulty passing exams
- Delay in program completion
- Potential dismissal

### Financial Consequences
- No refund for classes missed
- May owe additional tuition for repeat
- Loss of investment

### Career Consequences
- Poor habits carry to employment
- References may mention attendance
- Certification requirements may not be met

---

## Externship Attendance

Externship has stricter requirements:

- **100% attendance required**
- No make-up time available
- Excessive absence = reassignment or dismissal
- Must complete all required hours/competencies
