# Graduation Requirements

---

## General Requirements

To graduate from any program, students must:

| Requirement | Standard |
|-------------|----------|
| Complete all coursework | 100% of curriculum |
| Minimum attendance | 80% overall |
| Minimum GPA | 2.0 cumulative |
| All courses passed | Grade of C (70%) or higher |
| Financial obligations | Account current or paid |
| Complete externship | 100% of required hours (if applicable) |

---

## Program-Specific Requirements

### Medical Assistant

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Classroom hours | 640 hours minimum |
| Externship hours | 160 hours required |
| All competencies | Demonstrated and documented |
| Final GPA | 2.0 or higher |
| Financial status | Paid in full or payment current |

**Upon Completion:**
- Diploma/Certificate issued
- Eligible for NCMA, CCMA, or RMA certification exam

### Phlebotomy Technician

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Didactic hours | Complete per curriculum |
| Clinical requirements | 50 venipunctures, 10 skin punctures |
| Competency evaluation | Pass all clinical competencies |
| Final GPA | 2.0 or higher |
| Financial status | Paid in full or payment current |

**Upon Completion:**
- Diploma/Certificate issued
- Eligible for national certification exam
- May apply for CDPH CPT I certification

### Medical Coding Specialist

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Program hours | 460 hours minimum (80% attendance) |
| All modules | Completed with C or higher |
| Final comprehensive exam | Pass with 70% or higher |
| Coding practice | Required coding exercises completed |
| Financial status | Paid in full or payment current |

**Upon Completion:**
- Diploma/Certificate issued
- Eligible for CPC or CCA certification exam

### EKG Technician

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Program hours | 50 hours minimum (80% attendance) |
| Practical competencies | All EKG procedures demonstrated |
| Final exam | Pass with 70% or higher |
| Financial status | Paid in full or payment current |

**Upon Completion:**
- Certificate issued
- Eligible for CET certification exam

## Credential Issuance

### Timeline
- Credentials issued within 30 days of completion
- All requirements must be verified
- Financial account must be resolved

### Types of Credentials

| Credential | Issued For |
|------------|------------|
| Diploma | Medical Assistant, Medical Coding |
| Certificate | Phlebotomy, EKG Technician |

### Credential Replacement
- Replacement fee: $25
- Submit written request
- Processing time: 5-7 business days

## Exit Process

Before receiving credentials:

1. **Complete Exit Survey**
   - Program evaluation
   - Instructor feedback
   - Career readiness assessment

2. **Career Services Appointment**
   - Resume review
   - Job search resources
   - Certification exam information

3. **Financial Clearance**
   - Verify account status
   - Return any borrowed items
   - Update contact information

4. **Documentation**
   - Provide updated address for mailing
   - Sign release forms (if applicable)
   - Request transcripts (optional)

---

## Holds on Credentials

Credentials may be withheld for:
- Outstanding financial balance
- Unreturned school property
- Incomplete exit requirements
- Pending disciplinary matter
