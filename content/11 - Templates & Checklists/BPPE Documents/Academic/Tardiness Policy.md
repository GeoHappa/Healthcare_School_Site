# Tardiness Policy

---

## Definition

**Tardy:** Arriving more than 10 minutes after class start time OR leaving more than 10 minutes before class end time.

---

## Tardy Conversion

**Three (3) tardies = One (1) absence**

This conversion is applied automatically and affects attendance percentage.

---

## Tardy Tracking

| Occurrence | Action |
|------------|--------|
| 1st Tardy | Documented, verbal reminder |
| 2nd Tardy | Documented, verbal warning |
| 3rd Tardy | Converts to absence, written notice |
| 4th-5th Tardy | Documented for next conversion |
| 6th Tardy | Second absence conversion |

---

## Early Departure

Leaving class early follows the same policy:
- More than 10 minutes early = tardy
- Must notify instructor before leaving
- Sign out with departure time

---

## Exceptions

Tardiness may be excused with documentation for:
- Medical appointment (doctor's note)
- Court appearance (court documentation)
- Emergency (documentation required)

*Excused tardies do not count toward conversion*

---

## Impact on Grades

Consistent tardiness may also affect:
- Participation/professionalism grade
- Instructor recommendations
- Externship placement priority

---

## Tips for On-Time Arrival

1. Arrive 10-15 minutes early
2. Account for traffic/parking
3. Prepare materials night before
4. Set multiple alarms
5. Have backup transportation plan
