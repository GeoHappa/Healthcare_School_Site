## Phlebotomy Technician

### California Requirements

> **IMPORTANT DISCLOSURE:** In California, phlebotomists MUST be certified by the California Department of Public Health (CDPH) to perform venipuncture in licensed healthcare facilities.

### Steps to CDPH Certification

1. **Complete Approved Training** (This program meets requirements)
2. **Pass National Certification Exam** (from CDPH-approved agency)
3. **Apply to CDPH** for CPT I certificate
4. **Receive CPT I** certificate from CDPH

### CDPH-Approved Certifying Agencies

| Agency | Exam | Cost |
|--------|------|------|
| ASCP | PBT(ASCP) | ~$135 |
| NCCT | NCPT | ~$115 |
| AMT | RPT | ~$130 |
| NHA | CPT | ~$125 |

### CDPH Application

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Application Fee | $100 |
| Processing Time | 4-6 weeks |
| Renewal | Every 2 years |
| Renewal Fee | ~$100 |
| CE Required | Yes (6 hours per renewal period) |

### Employment Limitations

Without CDPH CPT I certification:
- Cannot perform venipuncture in licensed healthcare facilities
- May work in some non-licensed settings
- May work under direct supervision in training capacity

## Medical Coding Specialist

### California Requirements

> **DISCLOSURE:** California does NOT require Medical Coders to be licensed or certified.

### National Certification (Recommended)

While not required, certification is highly recommended:

| Credential | Organization | Exam Cost | Notes |
|------------|--------------|-----------|-------|
| CPC | AAPC | ~$399 | Most recognized; membership required |
| CCA | AHIMA | ~$299 | Alternative pathway |

### AAPC Certification Details

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Membership Required | Yes ($175/year) |
| Exam Format | 100 questions, 5 hrs 40 min |
| Passing Score | 70% |
| Initial Credential | CPC-A (Apprentice) |
| Full CPC | After 2 years experience |

### Employment Without Certification

- Entry-level positions may be available without certification
- Most employers prefer or require CPC or CCA
- Certification typically required for remote positions
- Higher pay associated with certification
