# Student's Right to Cancel

---

## Overview

Students have the right to cancel their enrollment agreement and obtain a refund of charges paid through attendance at the first class session, or the seventh day after enrollment, whichever is later. This page explains your cancellation rights as required by California law.

---

## Your Right to Cancel

### Timeframe

You have the right to cancel your enrollment agreement and receive a full refund before:
- The **first class session**, OR
- **Seven (7) days** after signing the enrollment agreement

**Whichever is later.**

### How to Cancel

To cancel, you must:
1. Provide **written notice** of cancellation
2. Submit the notice to the school at the address below
3. Keep a copy for your records

## Refund Upon Cancellation

### If You Cancel Within the Cancellation Period

You will receive a **full refund** of all charges paid, including:
- Tuition
- Registration fee
- STRF fee
- Any other charges

### Refund Timeline

Refunds will be issued within:
- **45 days** of receiving your cancellation notice
- **45 days** of the cancellation deadline if you do not attend

---

## After the Cancellation Period

### Withdrawal After Cancellation Period

If you withdraw after the cancellation period, refunds are calculated based on:
- Hours scheduled vs. hours completed
- Pro-rata calculation per [[Refund Policy]]

### No Penalty for Cancellation

There is **no penalty** for canceling during the cancellation period. You are entitled to a full refund regardless of reason.

## Important Notices

### Equipment and Materials

If you received equipment or materials:
- Return unused items within 10 days
- Materials must be in original condition
- Deductions may apply for used items

### Books

Books issued may be:
- Returned for full credit if unused
- Subject to deduction if opened/used
