- Industry trends

- Availability for work
- Transportation
- Schedule flexibility
- Willingness to relocate

## Placement Rate Disclosure

### How Placement Rates Are Calculated

Placement rates reported in the School Performance Fact Sheet are calculated according to BPPE requirements:

```
Placement Rate = Graduates Employed in Field รท Graduates Available for Employment ร 100
```

### Definitions

**Employed in Field:**
Graduates who obtained employment in a position related to the training within 6 months of graduation

**Available for Employment:**
Graduates who:
- Completed the program
- Are seeking employment in the field
- Are not continuing education

### Limitations

Placement rates may not reflect:
- Current job market conditions
- Individual circumstances
- Starting salaries
- Job satisfaction
- Length of employment
