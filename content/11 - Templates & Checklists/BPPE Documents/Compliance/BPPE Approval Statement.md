## What BPPE Approval Means

Approval to operate means the institution is compliant with the minimum standards contained in the California Private Postsecondary Education Act of 2009 (as amended) and Division 7.5 of Title 5 of the California Code of Regulations.

**BPPE approval indicates:**
- The school meets state educational standards
- The school has demonstrated financial stability
- The school maintains required records and disclosures
- The school has appropriate facilities and equipment
- Faculty meet minimum qualification requirements

---

## What BPPE Approval Does NOT Mean

> **IMPORTANT:** BPPE approval does not imply endorsement, recommendation, or approval of any program or course by the Bureau or the State of California.

BPPE approval does NOT mean:
- The programs are accredited
- Graduates will pass certification exams
- Graduates will find employment
- Credits will transfer to other institutions
- The school is recommended by the state

## Student Rights Under BPPE

Students enrolled in BPPE-approved schools have the right to:

1. **Receive Required Documents**
   - School Catalog
   - School Performance Fact Sheet
   - Enrollment Agreement

2. **Cancel Enrollment**
   - Per the refund policy in the enrollment agreement

3. **File Complaints**
   - Through the school's grievance procedure
   - With BPPE if unresolved

4. **STRF Protection**
   - If eligible per STRF requirements
