## Transcript Information Included

Official transcripts include:
- Student name and ID
- Program of study
- Dates of attendance
- Courses completed with grades
- Clock/credit hours
- Cumulative GPA
- Credential awarded (if applicable)
- Graduation date (if applicable)
- School seal and registrar signature

### Mail
- Standard USPS mail (included in fee)

### Record Retention
- Academic records maintained indefinitely
- Transcripts available permanently

### Updates to Records
- Name changes require legal documentation
- Grade corrections follow Grade Appeal policy
- Contact Registrar for record corrections

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How long are transcripts valid?**
A: Transcripts do not expire, but some recipients may require transcripts issued within a certain timeframe.

**Q: Can someone else pick up my transcript?**
A: Yes, with written authorization and their photo ID.

**Q: My name has changed. What do I need?**
A: Provide legal documentation (marriage certificate, court order) to update records before requesting transcripts.

**Q: I attended years ago. Can I still get transcripts?**
A: Yes, records are maintained indefinitely.
