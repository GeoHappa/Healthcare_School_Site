# Disability Services (ADA)

---

## Commitment to Accessibility

[SCHOOL NAME] is committed to providing equal educational opportunities to students with disabilities in compliance with Section 504 of the Rehabilitation Act and the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA).

---

## Who Qualifies

A person with a disability is someone who:
- Has a physical or mental impairment that substantially limits one or more major life activities
- Has a record of such impairment
- Is regarded as having such impairment

### Examples of Qualifying Disabilities
- Learning disabilities
- Attention deficit disorders
- Physical disabilities
- Sensory impairments (vision, hearing)
- Chronic health conditions
- Psychological disorders
- Autism spectrum disorders

---

## Available Accommodations

Reasonable accommodations may include but are not limited to:

### Testing Accommodations
- Extended time on exams
- Separate testing room
- Reader/scribe services
- Use of assistive technology

### Classroom Accommodations
- Preferential seating
- Note-taking assistance
- Recording of lectures
- Enlarged print materials
- Additional breaks

### Physical Accommodations
- Accessible classroom location
- Adjustable furniture
- Mobility assistance

### Other Accommodations
- Flexibility in attendance (documented medical needs)
- Modified assignments (when essential functions maintained)
- Communication accommodations

---

## What Cannot Be Accommodated

We cannot:
- Fundamentally alter program requirements
- Waive essential functions of healthcare training
- Lower academic or clinical standards
- Provide personal devices or attendants
- Accommodate requests that pose a direct threat to safety

---

## How to Request Accommodations

### Step 1: Contact Student Services
- Notify intent to request accommodations
- Obtain accommodation request form
- Discuss general process

### Step 2: Provide Documentation
Documentation must:
- Be from a qualified professional
- State the specific disability
- Describe functional limitations
- Recommend specific accommodations
- Be recent (within 3 years for most conditions)

### Step 3: Interactive Process
- Meet with Disability Services Coordinator
- Discuss requested accommodations
- Determine reasonable accommodations
- Review essential program requirements

### Step 4: Accommodation Plan
- Receive written accommodation plan
- Share plan with instructors (as needed)
- Implement accommodations
- Review and adjust as needed

---

## Timeline

| Step | Timeframe |
|------|-----------|
| Submit request | Before program start (recommended) |
| Review documentation | 5-7 business days |
| Develop plan | 5-7 business days |
| Implementation | Immediately upon approval |

**Note:** Accommodations are not retroactive. Request early.

---

## Student Responsibilities

Students with accommodations must:
- Provide documentation in a timely manner
- Meet with instructors to discuss accommodations
- Follow proper procedures for testing accommodations
- Notify staff if accommodations are not being implemented
- Maintain communication with Disability Services
- Meet the same academic standards as other students

---

## Confidentiality

- Disability information is confidential
- Shared only with those who need to know
- Instructors receive accommodation plan, not diagnosis
- Records maintained separately from academic file
- Protected under FERPA

---

## Grievance Procedure

If you believe you have been discriminated against:

1. Contact Disability Services Coordinator
2. If unresolved, file written complaint with School Director
3. External complaint: U.S. Department of Education, Office for Civil Rights

See [[../Policies/Grievance Procedure]] for details.

## Service Animals

### Policy
Service animals are permitted in all areas where students are allowed.

### Definition
A service animal is a dog (or miniature horse in some cases) individually trained to perform tasks for a person with a disability.

### Not Considered Service Animals
- Emotional support animals (ESAs)
- Therapy animals
- Pets

### Handler Responsibilities
- Animal under control at all times
- Animal housebroken
- Handler responsible for care and supervision
