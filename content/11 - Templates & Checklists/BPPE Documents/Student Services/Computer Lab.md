## Rules and Policies

### General Rules

1. **No food or drinks** near computers
2. **Sign in/out** for each visit
3. **Log off** when finished
4. **Report problems** immediately
5. **Respect equipment** - handle carefully
6. **Keep noise low** - use headphones for audio
7. **Save work frequently** - to your own storage

### Prohibited Actions

- Changing computer settings
- Installing software
- Downloading unauthorized programs
- Tampering with hardware
- Accessing others' accounts
- Sharing login credentials

### Consequences

| Violation | Consequence |
|-----------|-------------|
| Minor rule violation | Warning |
| Repeated violations | Loss of lab privileges |
| Damage to equipment | Repair/replacement cost |
| Serious policy violation | Disciplinary action |
