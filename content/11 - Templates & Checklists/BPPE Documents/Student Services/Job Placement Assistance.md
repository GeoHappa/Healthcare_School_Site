## Factors Affecting Placement

### You Can Control
- Resume quality
- Interview preparation
- Certification status
- Flexibility on location/schedule
- Application volume
- Networking effort
- Professional presentation

### External Factors
- Local job market
- Economic conditions

## Tips for Success

### Be Proactive
- Don't wait for graduation to start
- Apply broadly, then narrow
- Follow up on applications
- Network continuously

### Be Flexible
- Consider various settings
- Be open to different schedules
- Look beyond immediate area
- Consider entry-level positions

### Be Prepared
- Complete certification exams promptly
- Keep documents current
- Practice interviews
- Maintain professional appearance

### Be Persistent
- Job searching is a job itself
- Don't get discouraged
- Learn from rejections
- Stay positive
