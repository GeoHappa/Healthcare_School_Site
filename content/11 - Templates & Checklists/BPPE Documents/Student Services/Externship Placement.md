## Placement Process

### Timeline

| Stage | Timing |
|-------|--------|
| Eligibility verification | 4 weeks before externship start |
| Site matching | 2-3 weeks before |
| Site assignment | 1-2 weeks before |
| Orientation | Week before or first day |
| Begin externship | Per program schedule |

### How Sites Are Assigned

Placement considers:
1. **Program requirements** - Site meets learning objectives
2. **Student schedule** - Availability matches site hours
3. **Geography** - Reasonable distance for student
4. **Site capacity** - Available student slots
5. **Student preferences** - When possible
6. **Site preferences** - If specified

### Notification

You will receive:
- Written site assignment

Contact Clinical Coordinator immediately if:

## Before Your First Day

### Preparation Checklist

- [ ] Know site address and parking
- [ ] Know your start time
- [ ] Have supervisor contact information
- [ ] Know dress code requirements
- [ ] Have required documents ready
- [ ] Plan your route
- [ ] Set multiple alarms

### Documents to Bring

- Photo ID
- CPR card
- Clinical clearance packet (if required)
- School contact information
- Emergency contact information

### What to Wear

Unless otherwise specified:
- Clean, pressed scrubs
- Closed-toe shoes
- Name badge
- Watch with second hand
- Minimal jewelry

## First Day

### Arrival

- Arrive 15 minutes early
- Check in at reception/HR as instructed
- Introduce yourself professionally
- Complete any site orientation

### Orientation May Include

- Facility tour
- Introduction to staff
- Review of policies
- EMR training
- Safety procedures
- Schedule review

### First Day Tips

- Be professional and positive
- Listen carefully
- Ask questions
- Take notes
- Observe first, then participate

---

## During Externship

### Your Schedule

- Follow assigned schedule exactly
- Arrive on time every day
- Stay entire shift
- Take breaks as permitted
- Don't leave early without approval

### Daily Responsibilities

| Task | Frequency |
|------|-----------|
| Sign in | Daily |
| Complete assigned duties | Ongoing |
| Document hours on timesheet | Daily |
| Meet with supervisor | As scheduled |
| Complete competencies | As opportunities arise |

### Supervision

- Always work under supervision
- Never perform procedures alone (until approved)
- Ask before doing unfamiliar tasks
- Report concerns immediately

## Problems at Site

### What to Do

If you experience:

- Unsafe conditions
- Inadequate supervision
