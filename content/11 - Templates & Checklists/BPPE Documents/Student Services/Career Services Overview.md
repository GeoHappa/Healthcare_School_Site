## Success Tips

1. **Start Early** - Begin career prep during the program, not after
2. **Attend Workshops** - Take advantage of all available training
3. **Update Resume** - Keep current with new skills and experience
4. **Network** - Build professional connections during externship
5. **Stay Connected** - Keep Career Services informed of job search
6. **Be Flexible** - Consider various opportunities
7. **Follow Up** - Respond promptly to leads and referrals
