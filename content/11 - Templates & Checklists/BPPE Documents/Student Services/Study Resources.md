## Recommended Study Strategies

### Active Learning Techniques
1. **Teach Back** - Explain concepts to someone else
2. **Practice Testing** - Take practice quizzes frequently
3. **Spaced Repetition** - Review material over time
4. **Interleaving** - Mix up topics while studying
5. **Elaboration** - Connect new concepts to known ones

### Time Management
1. Create a weekly study schedule
2. Study in focused blocks (25-50 minutes)
3. Take short breaks
4. Review notes within 24 hours of class
5. Don't cram - distribute practice

### For Skills Mastery
1. Watch demonstrations first
2. Practice on equipment
3. Use checklists
4. Get feedback from instructors
5. Practice until automatic

---

## Creating a Study Plan

### Weekly Planning
1. Review upcoming exams and assignments
2. Block study time in calendar
3. Prioritize challenging material
4. Include review of previous content
5. Schedule breaks

### Before Exams
1. Start review 1-2 weeks early
2. Use practice tests to identify gaps
3. Focus on weak areas
4. Get adequate sleep before exam
5. Review morning of exam (briefly)
