## Getting the Most from Tutoring

### Before Your Session
1. Identify specific topics or questions
2. Bring textbooks and notes
3. Review material beforehand
4. List what you don't understand

### During Your Session
1. Ask questions
2. Take notes
3. Practice problems/skills
4. Request examples
5. Be honest about confusion

### After Your Session
1. Review notes within 24 hours
2. Practice what you learned
3. Complete assigned exercises
4. Prepare questions for next session

## When to Seek Tutoring

### Recommended Situations
- Struggling with specific concepts
- Preparing for certification exams
- Falling behind in coursework
- Returning from absence
- Before skills checkoffs
- When grades drop

### Early Intervention
Don't wait until you're failing. Seek help when:
- You don't understand lectures
- Homework takes much longer than expected
- Quiz scores are below 75%
- You feel confused or frustrated

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How often can I use tutoring?**
A: As often as needed. There is no limit on tutoring sessions.

**Q: What if I need help outside tutoring hours?**
A: Email your instructor or request a special appointment through Student Services.

**Q: Can I get tutoring for certification exam prep?**
A: Yes! We offer specific exam prep sessions for all certification exams.

**Q: Is tutoring mandatory?**
A: Tutoring is voluntary unless you are on academic warning or probation.
