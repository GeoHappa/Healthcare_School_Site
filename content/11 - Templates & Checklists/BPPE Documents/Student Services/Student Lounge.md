## Guidelines

### Food and Beverages

**Permitted:**
- Packed lunches and snacks
- Beverages in closed containers
- Microwave-safe meals
- Snacks from vending machines

**Not Permitted:**
- Cooking (beyond microwave)
- Alcohol
- Open flames or heating elements
- Strong-smelling foods (be considerate)

### Refrigerator Use

- Label all items with name and date
- Remove items by end of week
- Unlabeled items may be discarded
- Clean up any spills immediately
- Do not store items long-term

### Microwave Use

- Cover food when heating
- Clean up splatters immediately
- Use microwave-safe containers only
- Time limits during busy periods
- Report malfunctions

---

## Rules and Expectations

### General Rules

1. **Clean up after yourself** - Dispose of trash, wipe tables
2. **Be considerate** - Keep noise reasonable
3. **Share space** - Don't monopolize tables or outlets
4. **Respect others** - Maintain professional atmosphere
5. **No studying during peak lunch hours** - Reserve tables for eating
6. **Report issues** - Broken equipment, messes, concerns

### Study vs. Eating

| Time Period | Primary Use |
|-------------|-------------|
| Between classes | Both study and eating |
| Lunch period (11am-1pm) | Eating - study in library |
| Before/after school | Both |

### Noise Level

- Conversational voices appropriate
- No loud music without headphones
- Be mindful during others' study time
- Phone calls - step outside or keep brief
