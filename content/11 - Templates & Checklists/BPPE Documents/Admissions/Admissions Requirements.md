# Admissions Requirements

---

## General Requirements (All Programs)

### Age Requirement
- Minimum 18 years of age at time of enrollment
- No maximum age limit

### Education Requirement
- High school diploma, OR
- General Educational Development (GED) certificate, OR
- Equivalent foreign credential (must be evaluated)

### Documentation Required
- Valid government-issued photo ID (driver's license, state ID, passport)
- Proof of high school completion or GED
- Social Security card or documentation of legal presence

### Language Proficiency
- Ability to read, write, and speak English
- All instruction conducted in English
- No ESL services available

---

## Clinical Program Requirements

Programs with patient contact (Medical Assistant, Phlebotomy, EKG) require:

### Background Check
- National criminal background check
- Must be cleared before clinical placement
- Cost: Approximately $50-75 (student responsibility)
- Processing time: 3-5 business days

**Disqualifying Offenses May Include:**
- Felony convictions (case-by-case review)
- Crimes involving violence
- Drug-related offenses
- Theft or fraud
- Crimes against vulnerable populations

*Note: A criminal record does not automatically disqualify an applicant. Each case reviewed individually.*

### Drug Screening
- 10-panel drug screen required
- Must be negative for clinical placement
- Cost: Approximately $35-50 (student responsibility)
- Random testing may occur during program

### Health Requirements

**Immunizations Required:**
| Vaccine | Requirement |
|---------|-------------|
| Hepatitis B | 3-dose series OR signed declination |
| MMR | 2 doses OR titer showing immunity |
| Varicella | 2 doses OR titer OR documented history |
| Tdap | Within last 10 years |
| Influenza | Current season (during flu season) |

**Tuberculosis Screening:**
- TB skin test (PPD) or blood test (QuantiFERON)
- Must be completed within 12 months
- If positive, chest X-ray required

**Physical Examination:**
- May be required by clinical sites
- Must be able to perform essential functions

### CPR/BLS Certification
- American Heart Association BLS for Healthcare Providers
- OR American Red Cross BLS for Healthcare Providers
- Must be current throughout program
- May be obtained during program (before externship)

---

## Program-Specific Requirements

### Medical Assistant Program
All clinical requirements above, plus:
- Manual dexterity assessment
- Color vision assessment (for lab work)
- Ability to stand for extended periods

### Phlebotomy Technician Program
All clinical requirements above, plus:
- Fine motor skills assessment
- Ability to perform venipuncture techniques
- No latex allergy (or ability to use alternatives)

### Medical Coding Specialist Program
- Computer proficiency required
- Typing speed: Minimum 35 WPM recommended
- Basic math skills
- Attention to detail assessment
- No clinical requirements (no patient contact)

### EKG Technician Program
- Background check required
- Basic clinical requirements
- Ability to operate electronic equipment

---

## Essential Functions

All students must be able to perform essential functions with or without reasonable accommodation:

### Physical Requirements
- Stand and walk for extended periods
- Lift up to 25 pounds
- Use fine motor skills for procedures
- Reach, bend, and stoop as needed

### Sensory Requirements
- Visual acuity for reading and procedures
- Color discrimination (clinical programs)
- Hearing adequate for patient communication
- Tactile sensitivity for clinical procedures

### Cognitive Requirements
- Follow written and verbal instructions
- Perform calculations
- Make appropriate judgments
- Communicate effectively

### Professional Requirements
- Maintain professional demeanor
- Work with diverse populations
- Handle stressful situations
- Maintain confidentiality

## Accommodation Requests

Students requiring accommodations should:
1. Contact Student Services
2. Provide documentation of disability
3. Meet to discuss appropriate accommodations
4. Receive written accommodation plan

See [[../Policies/ADA Accommodations Policy]] for details.

---

## Verification Process

All documentation is verified by the Admissions Office:
- Education credentials verified with issuing institution
- Background checks through approved vendor
- Health records reviewed by clinical coordinator
- International credentials evaluated by approved agency
