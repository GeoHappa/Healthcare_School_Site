## Step-by-Step Application Process

### Step 1: Initial Inquiry

**Contact Admissions:**
- Call: [PHONE]
- Email: [EMAIL]
- Visit: [ADDRESS]
- Online: [WEBSITE]

**What to Expect:**
- Brief introduction to programs
- Overview of requirements
- Schedule campus tour
- Receive information packet

---

### Step 2: Campus Tour & Information Session

**Duration:** Approximately 1 hour

**Includes:**
- Tour of classroom and lab facilities
- Meet with admissions representative
- Review program curriculum
- Discuss career opportunities
- Q&A session

**Schedule Options:**
- Monday-Friday: [HOURS]
- Saturday: By appointment
- Virtual tours available

---

### Step 3: Complete Application

**Application Components:**

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Application Form | Complete all sections |
| Application Fee | $[AMOUNT] (non-refundable) |
| Photo ID | Copy of valid government ID |
| Education Proof | Diploma, GED, or transcripts |

**How to Submit:**
- In person at campus
- Email to [EMAIL]
- Mail to [ADDRESS]

---

### Step 4: Admissions Interview

**Purpose:**
- Assess motivation and goals
- Review program expectations
- Answer applicant questions
- Determine program fit

**Interview Topics:**
- Career goals
- Understanding of healthcare field
- Commitment to program completion
- Schedule availability
- Financial planning

---

### Step 5: Entrance Assessment

**Assessment Components:**

| Area | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Reading | Comprehension and vocabulary |
| Math | Basic calculations |
| Writing | Grammar and composition |

**Minimum Scores:**
- Varies by program
- Results available same day
- May retake after 7 days

**Study Resources:**
- Study guide provided upon request
- Online practice available
- Tutoring referrals if needed

---

### Step 6: Review Required Documents

**Receive and Review:**
- School Catalog
- School Performance Fact Sheet
- Enrollment Agreement
- Program-specific disclosures

**Important:**
- Take documents home to review
- Minimum 24-hour cooling-off period
- Ask questions before signing

---

### Step 7: Enrollment Meeting

**Complete the Following:**
- Sign Enrollment Agreement
- Review and sign disclosure documents
- Complete financial arrangements
- Schedule program start date
- Receive student ID and materials

## Denied Applications

If an application is denied:
- Written notification provided
- Reason for denial explained
- Appeal process available
- May reapply after 6 months

**Common Denial Reasons:**
- Incomplete documentation
- Failed entrance assessment
- Background check issues
- Health clearance concerns
