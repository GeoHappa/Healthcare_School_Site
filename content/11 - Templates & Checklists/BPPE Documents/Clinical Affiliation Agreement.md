## RECITALS

**WHEREAS,** School is a healthcare training institution offering educational programs in healthcare disciplines; and

**WHEREAS,** Facility is a healthcare facility capable of providing clinical training experiences; and

**WHEREAS,** both parties desire to cooperate in providing clinical education experiences for students enrolled in School's programs; and

**WHEREAS,** both parties believe such cooperation will benefit students, patients, and the community;

**NOW, THEREFORE,** in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements contained herein, the parties agree as follows:

---

## ARTICLE I: PURPOSE

### 1.1 Purpose

The purpose of this Agreement is to establish the terms and conditions under which School's students ("Students") will receive clinical education and training at Facility under the supervision of qualified personnel.

### 1.2 Programs Covered

This Agreement covers the following educational program(s):

- [ ] Medical Assistant
- [ ] Phlebotomy Technician
- [ ] Medical Coding (if applicable)
- [ ] EKG Technician
- [ ] Other: _______________________

### 1.3 Non-Employee Status

Students are not employees of Facility and are not entitled to any compensation, benefits, or employee privileges from Facility for their clinical education activities.

---

## ARTICLE II: RESPONSIBILITIES OF SCHOOL

School agrees to:

### 2.1 Program Administration

a) Designate a faculty member or Program Director ("Clinical Coordinator") to coordinate with Facility

b) Provide curriculum information, learning objectives, and competency requirements

c) Establish academic requirements for clinical placement

d) Provide academic supervision and evaluation of Students

### 2.2 Student Preparation

a) Ensure Students have completed prerequisite didactic and laboratory instruction

b) Verify Students have required knowledge and skills before clinical placement

c) Provide orientation to clinical expectations and professional conduct

d) Ensure Students understand HIPAA requirements and confidentiality obligations

### 2.3 Student Documentation

Prior to clinical placement, provide documentation that Students have:

a) Current health clearance including:
   - Tuberculosis test (within 12 months)
   - Required immunizations per Facility policy
   - Physical examination (if required by Facility)

b) Current CPR/BLS certification (American Heart Association or equivalent)

c) Background check clearance (within 12 months)

d) Drug screening (if required by Facility)

e) Professional liability insurance coverage

f) Signed HIPAA confidentiality agreement

### 2.4 Student Supervision

a) Ensure Students are supervised at all times during clinical activities

b) Provide clinical instruction as agreed with Facility

c) Make reasonable site visits during clinical rotations

d) Be available to Facility for consultation regarding Student issues

### 2.5 Compliance

a) Maintain all required licenses and approvals (BPPE, accreditation)

b) Ensure program curriculum meets industry standards

c) Comply with all applicable laws and regulations

d) Carry general liability insurance (minimum $1,000,000 per occurrence)

### 2.6 Removal of Students

a) Remove any Student from Facility upon Facility's request

b) Address Student performance or conduct issues promptly

c) Cooperate with Facility investigations as needed

---

## ARTICLE III: RESPONSIBILITIES OF FACILITY

Facility agrees to:

### 3.1 Clinical Experience

a) Provide clinical learning experiences appropriate to program objectives

b) Make available space, equipment, and supplies for clinical activities

c) Provide orientation to Facility policies and procedures

d) Allow access to patient care areas as appropriate for training

### 3.2 Supervision

a) Designate a clinical supervisor ("Site Coordinator") to coordinate with School

b) Provide qualified personnel to supervise Students during clinical activities

c) Ensure Students perform only activities within their scope of training

d) Evaluate Student performance using School's evaluation tools

### 3.3 Patient Care Standards

a) Maintain responsibility for patient care at all times

b) Ensure Student activities are appropriate for patient safety

c) Maintain standards of care regardless of Student participation

d) Retain authority over all clinical decisions

### 3.4 Communication

a) Notify School promptly of any Student performance or conduct concerns

b) Provide feedback on Student progress as requested

c) Communicate any changes in Facility policies affecting Students

d) Participate in program evaluation as reasonably requested

### 3.5 Removal Authority

a) Facility may remove any Student from clinical placement at any time

b) Facility will provide reason for removal to School

c) No prior notice required if patient safety or Facility operations at risk

## ARTICLE V: STUDENTS

### 5.1 Student Status

Students shall:

a) Not be considered employees of Facility

b) Not be entitled to compensation from Facility

c) Not be entitled to employee benefits from Facility

d) Remain students of School throughout clinical rotation

### 5.2 Student Responsibilities

Students shall:

a) Follow all Facility policies and procedures

b) Maintain professional conduct and appearance

c) Maintain patient confidentiality (HIPAA)

d) Perform only activities within scope of training and supervision

e) Report to clinical assignments as scheduled

f) Complete all required documentation

g) Report any incidents or injuries immediately

### 5.3 Student Records

a) Student records maintained by School per FERPA

b) Facility may access records as necessary for clinical placement

c) Students may access their clinical evaluations per School policy

---

## ARTICLE VI: INSURANCE AND LIABILITY

### 6.1 School Insurance

School shall maintain:

a) **General Liability Insurance:** Minimum $1,000,000 per occurrence, $3,000,000 aggregate

b) **Professional Liability Insurance:** Minimum $1,000,000 per occurrence, $3,000,000 aggregate (covering Students during clinical activities)

c) **Workers' Compensation Insurance:** As required by California law (for School employees)

### 6.2 Facility Insurance

Facility shall maintain:

a) **General Liability Insurance:** Minimum $1,000,000 per occurrence

b) **Professional Liability Insurance:** Minimum $1,000,000 per occurrence

c) **Workers' Compensation Insurance:** As required by California law

### 6.3 Proof of Insurance

Each party shall provide proof of insurance upon request.

### 6.4 Student Coverage

Students are covered under School's professional liability insurance while engaged in clinical activities under this Agreement.

### 6.5 Student Injuries

a) Students are not covered by Facility's workers' compensation

b) School will inform Students they are responsible for their own health insurance

c) Students acknowledge risk of injury during clinical activities

---

## ARTICLE VII: INDEMNIFICATION

### 7.1 School Indemnification

School agrees to indemnify, defend, and hold harmless Facility, its officers, directors, employees, and agents from and against any claims, damages, losses, or expenses arising from:

a) Negligent acts or omissions of School or its employees

b) Negligent acts or omissions of Students during clinical activities

c) Breach of this Agreement by School

### 7.2 Facility Indemnification

Facility agrees to indemnify, defend, and hold harmless School, its officers, directors, employees, and agents from and against any claims, damages, losses, or expenses arising from:

a) Negligent acts or omissions of Facility or its employees

b) Breach of this Agreement by Facility

### 7.3 Notification

Each party shall promptly notify the other of any claim or action arising under this Agreement.

---

## ARTICLE VIII: TERM AND TERMINATION

### 8.1 Term

This Agreement shall be effective as of the Effective Date and shall continue for a period of **three (3) years** unless earlier terminated.

### 8.2 Renewal

This Agreement shall automatically renew for successive one (1) year periods unless either party provides written notice of intent not to renew at least sixty (60) days prior to expiration.

### 8.3 Termination Without Cause

Either party may terminate this Agreement without cause by providing ninety (90) days written notice to the other party.

### 8.4 Termination for Cause

Either party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if the other party:

a) Materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure within thirty (30) days of notice

b) Loses required licenses or certifications

c) Becomes insolvent or files for bankruptcy

### 8.5 Effect of Termination

Upon termination:

a) Students currently in clinical rotation may complete their rotation unless unsafe

b) All confidential information shall be returned or destroyed

c) No new Students shall be placed at Facility

d) Obligations regarding indemnification and confidentiality survive termination

## ARTICLE IX: GENERAL PROVISIONS

### 9.1 Independent Contractor

The relationship between School and Facility is that of independent contractors. Nothing in this Agreement creates an employment, partnership, or joint venture relationship.

### 9.2 Assignment

Neither party may assign this Agreement without prior written consent of the other party.

### 9.3 Amendment

This Agreement may be amended only by written agreement signed by both parties.

### 9.4 Waiver

Failure to enforce any provision of this Agreement shall not constitute a waiver of that provision or any other provision.

### 9.5 Severability

If any provision of this Agreement is held invalid, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect.

### 9.6 Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior agreements and understandings.

### 9.7 Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of California.

### 9.8 Notices

All notices shall be in writing and sent to the addresses set forth above, or such other address as either party may designate in writing.

### 9.9 Counterparts

This Agreement may be executed in counterparts, each of which shall be deemed an original.
