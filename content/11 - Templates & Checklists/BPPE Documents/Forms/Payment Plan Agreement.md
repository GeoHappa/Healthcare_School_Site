## Payment Terms and Conditions

### 1. Interest Rate
This payment plan is **interest-free** when payments are made on time.

### 2. Due Dates
- Payments are due on the date specified in the schedule above
- Payments received after the due date are considered late

### 3. Grace Period
- A 5-day grace period is provided after each due date
- No late fee assessed during grace period

### 4. Late Fees
- **$25.00** late fee applied after the grace period
- Maximum late fees: $100 per program

### 5. Returned Payments
- **$35.00** fee for returned checks or declined payments
- Payment must be replaced within 5 business days

### 6. Payment Methods Accepted
- Cash
- Personal Check
- Money Order
- Credit Card (Visa, MasterCard, Discover, Amex)
- Debit Card
- ACH/Bank Transfer (for automatic payments)

## Default Terms

### Definition of Default
An account is in default when:
- Payment is 60+ days past due, OR
- Three or more payments are missed, OR
- Student stops attending without making arrangements

### Consequences of Default
- Suspension from classes
- Withholding of transcripts and credentials
- Account referred to collection agency
- Negative impact on credit history
- Legal action to recover balance
- Additional collection costs may be added to balance

---

## Early Payoff

- No penalty for early payoff
- Full balance may be paid at any time
- Partial prepayments applied to principal

## Changes to Payment Plan

To modify this payment plan:
1. Contact the Business Office before payment due date
2. Submit written request explaining circumstances
3. Modifications subject to approval by School Director
4. New agreement required for any changes
