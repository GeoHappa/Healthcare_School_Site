## Transfer Credit Guidelines

### Eligibility Criteria

To be considered for transfer:
- Coursework from accredited or BPPE-approved institution
- Grade of C (70%) or higher earned
- Completed within the last 5 years
- Content equivalent to [SCHOOL NAME] courses
- Maximum 25% of program hours transferable

### Evaluation Process

1. Submit complete request with transcripts
2. Program Director reviews within [X] business days
3. Student notified of decision
4. If approved, enrollment agreement adjusted
5. Tuition adjusted per [[Tuition Adjustments]]
