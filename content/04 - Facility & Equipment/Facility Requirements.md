# Facility Requirements

---

## Overview

The healthcare training center requires a professional, accessible facility that accommodates classroom instruction, clinical skills labs, computer labs, and administrative functions. This document provides comprehensive specifications for facility selection, layout, and compliance.

## Lease Negotiation Guide

### Standard Terms (Folsom Commercial)

| Factor | Typical Range |
|--------|---------------|
| Lease Length | 3-5 years |
| Base Rent | $1.80-$2.50/sq ft/month |
| NNN Expenses | $0.35-$0.60/sq ft/month |
| Annual Increases | 3-4% |
| Security Deposit | 2 months rent |

### Negotiation Priorities

1. **Free Rent Period**
   - Ask for 3-6 months during build-out/approval
   - Justify: No revenue until BPPE approved

2. **Tenant Improvement (TI) Allowance**
   - Ask for $10-20/sq ft
   - Or landlord-completed improvements

3. **Signage Rights**
   - Exterior building signage
   - Monument sign (if available)
   - Window/door signage

4. **Renewal Options**
   - 2-3 renewal options at set rates
   - Right of first refusal on adjacent space

5. **Early Termination**
   - 90-day notice with penalty
   - Protection if business closes

6. **Exclusivity Clause**
   - No competing vocational schools in building/complex
