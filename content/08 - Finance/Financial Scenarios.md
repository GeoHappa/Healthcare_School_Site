## Decision Framework

### Which Scenario to Plan For?

**Recommendation: Plan for Moderate, Prepare for Conservative**

| Planning Element | Use This Scenario |
|------------------|-------------------|
| Revenue targets | Moderate |
| Expense budget | Conservative |
| Cash reserves | Conservative |
| Marketing spend | Between Moderate/Aggressive |
| Hiring | Moderate (with contingency) |
| Facility size | Moderate (with expansion option) |

### Go/No-Go Criteria

**Proceed if:**
- [ ] Secured minimum $300K funding
- [ ] BPPE approval timeline < 8 months
- [ ] Identified 3+ clinical partners
- [ ] Qualified PD candidate available
- [ ] Facility lease signed

**Reconsider if:**
- [ ] Funding below $250K
- [ ] BPPE timeline > 10 months
- [ ] No clinical partners confirmed
- [ ] Market research shows low demand

## Important Notes

### STRF Assessment Rate
As of April 1, 2024, the STRF (Student Tuition Recovery Fund) assessment rate is **$0.00 per $1,000** of institutional charges. This represents a significant cost savings. All projections in this document reflect the current $0.00 rate.

**Monitor BPPE announcements for rate changes.** When the rate returns above $0.00, update all financial projections accordingly. Historical rate was $2.50 per $1,000 (January 2022 - March 2024).
