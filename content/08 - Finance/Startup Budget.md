## Funding Sources

### Potential Funding Options

| Source | Amount | Pros | Cons |
|--------|--------|------|------|
| Personal savings | Variable | No debt, full control | Limited by savings |
| SBA 7(a) loan | Up to $350K | Low rates, long terms | Requires collateral, takes time |
| SBA Microloan | Up to $50K | Easier qualification | Smaller amounts |
| Business line of credit | $50-100K | Flexible access | Higher rates |
| Private investors | Variable | No bank qualification | Give up equity |
| Equipment financing | Equipment cost | Preserves cash | Interest costs |
