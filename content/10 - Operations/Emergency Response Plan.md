## Fire Emergency

### If You Discover Fire or Smoke

1. **ALERT** - Pull nearest fire alarm
2. **EVACUATE** - Leave immediately via nearest exit
3. **CALL** - Call 911 from safe location
4. **ASSEMBLE** - Go to assembly point
5. **ACCOUNT** - Report to Director for headcount

### Evacuation Routes
- Posted at all classroom entrances
- Primary and alternate routes marked
- Emergency exits clearly labeled

### Fire Extinguisher Use (PASS Method)
- **P**ull the pin
- **A**im at base of fire
- **S**queeze the handle
- **S**weep side to side

**Only attempt to extinguish small fires if safe to do so. When in doubt, evacuate.**

### DO NOT
- Use elevators
- Re-enter building until cleared by fire department
- Prop open fire doors
- Block emergency exits

## Medical Emergency

### If Someone is Injured or Ill

1. **ASSESS** - Check for responsiveness and breathing
2. **CALL** - Call 911 for serious injuries
3. **AID** - Provide first aid if trained
4. **COMFORT** - Stay with person until help arrives
5. **DOCUMENT** - Complete incident report after

### First Aid Kit Locations
- Front desk reception area
- Clinical skills lab
- Break room

### AED Location
- [Location in facility]

### If Blood/Body Fluid Exposure
1. Remove contaminated clothing
2. Wash affected area immediately
3. Report to Program Director
4. Seek medical evaluation within 2 hours
5. Complete incident report

## Active Threat / Lockdown

### RUN - HIDE - FIGHT

#### RUN (If Safe Path Exists)
- Leave belongings behind
- Evacuate regardless of others
- Keep hands visible
- Call 911 when safe

#### HIDE (If Escape Not Possible)
- Find a room that locks
- Lock and barricade door
- Silence phones
- Hide behind large objects
- Turn off lights
- Stay away from windows
- Remain quiet

#### FIGHT (Only as Last Resort)
- Commit fully to action
- Use improvised weapons
- Act with aggression
- Do not negotiate

### When Law Enforcement Arrives
- Keep hands visible
- Follow all instructions
- Don't point or yell
- Evacuate in directed route

## Severe Weather

### Tornado Warning
1. Move to interior room/hallway
2. Stay away from windows
3. Get low and protect head
4. Remain until all-clear

### Earthquake
1. **DROP** - Get down on hands and knees
2. **COVER** - Get under desk/table
3. **HOLD ON** - Stay until shaking stops
4. After shaking: Check for injuries, evacuate if building damaged, avoid damaged areas

### Power Outage
1. Remain calm
2. Use flashlights (not candles)
3. Secure sensitive equipment
4. Evacuate if HVAC fails for extended period
5. Contact Facility Manager

## Bomb Threat

### If Threat Received by Phone
1. **Stay calm** - Keep caller on line as long as possible
2. **Signal** - Alert coworker to call 911
3. **Record** - Note exact words, voice characteristics, background sounds
4. **Ask** - Where is the bomb? When will it explode? What does it look like? Why?
5. **Document** - Complete bomb threat checklist immediately after

### If Threat Received by Other Means
1. Do NOT handle suspicious item
2. Evacuate area
3. Call 911
4. Do NOT use cell phones near suspicious item

### If Suspicious Package Found
- Do NOT touch, move, or open
- Evacuate immediate area
- Call 911
- Clear area of at least 300 feet

## Chemical Spill / Hazmat

### Minor Spill (Small, Known Substance)
1. Alert others in area
2. Open windows if ventilation needed
3. Use spill kit if trained
4. Clean with appropriate materials
5. Dispose properly

### Major Spill or Unknown Substance
1. Evacuate area immediately
2. Call 911
3. Seal off area if possible
4. Move assembly point UPWIND
5. Do NOT attempt cleanup

## Student Mental Health Crisis

### Signs of Distress
- Talking about self-harm or suicide
- Giving away possessions
- Expressing hopelessness
- Erratic behavior changes
- Substance use concerns

### Response Steps
1. **Stay calm** - Don't leave person alone
2. **Listen** - Take concerns seriously
3. **Ask directly** - "Are you thinking of hurting yourself?"
4. **Get help** - Contact Program Director
5. **If immediate danger** - Call 911

### Crisis Resources
- National Suicide Prevention: **988**
- Crisis Text Line: Text **HOME** to **741741**
