## Pricing Philosophy

### Core Principles

1. **Value-Based** - Price reflects quality of training and outcomes
2. **Competitive** - Positioned appropriately vs. competitors
3. **Accessible** - Payment options make education attainable
4. **Sustainable** - Covers costs and allows for growth
5. **Transparent** - No hidden fees, clear total costs

## WIOA Pricing Considerations

### WIOA ITA Limits

- Co-payment requirements

### Strategy

- Price within typical ITA ranges when possible
- Offer payment plan for amounts exceeding ITA
- Maintain relationships with workforce boards

## Price Communication

### Transparency Requirements

BPPE requires:
- Complete cost disclosure before enrollment
- Itemized fees in enrollment agreement
- No hidden costs

### Presentation

- Lead with value, not price
- Explain what's included
- Compare total cost (no surprises)
- Discuss payment options
- Address WIOA/funding early

---

## Price Adjustments

### When to Adjust Prices

Consider adjustment when:
- Costs increase significantly
- Market rates change
- Program content expands
- Value proposition changes
- Economic conditions shift

### How to Adjust

1. **Annual Review** - Assess each year
2. **Competitive Check** - Verify market position
3. **Cost Analysis** - Ensure profitability
4. **Gradual Changes** - Avoid large jumps
5. **Advance Notice** - Communicate to prospects
6. **Honor Existing** - Enrolled students not affected

### Price Increase Guidelines

- Maximum [X]% increase per year
- Document rationale
- Communicate changes in advance
- Update all materials
