# Program Opportunity Matrix

## Overview

This matrix evaluates potential program offerings based on market demand, competition, startup requirements, and profitability potential.

---

## Scoring Criteria

| Factor | Weight | Description |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Market Demand | 25% | Job openings, growth projections, employer need |
| Competition | 25% | Number of programs within 30 miles |
| Startup Cost | 20% | Equipment, curriculum, regulatory requirements |
| Profit Margin | 15% | Tuition vs. delivery cost |
| Time to Launch | 15% | Regulatory approval, curriculum development |

**Score Range:** 1 (Poor) to 5 (Excellent)
