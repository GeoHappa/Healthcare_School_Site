## Key Competitor Deep Dives

### Medical Career College of Northern California (7.0 mi)
**Location:** Roseville, CA
**Programs:** Medical Imaging, Allied Health
**Strengths:** Established brand, ultrasound focus
**Weaknesses:** Limited program variety
**Opportunity:** They don't offer MA, Phlebotomy, or Coding

### Carrington College (13.5 mi)
**Location:** Sacramento, CA
**Programs:** Allied Health, Dental, Accounting, Pharmacy Tech
**Strengths:** Large national brand, accredited, financial aid
**Weaknesses:** Higher tuition, corporate feel
**Opportunity:** Closer location would appeal to Folsom residents

### Campus Sacramento (9.1 mi)
**Location:** Sacramento, CA
**Programs:** Medical Assisting with Phlebotomy, IT, Legal
**Strengths:** Offers MA program, established
**Tuition:** $17,000 (MA) + $3,772 supplies = $20,772 total
**Weaknesses:** Not in Folsom, higher price point
**Opportunity:** Price and location advantage possible

### Chamberlain University (7.9 mi)
**Location:** Rancho Cordova, CA
**Programs:** BSN Nursing
**Strengths:** Nursing degree programs
**Weaknesses:** Degree-only (2-4 years), higher cost
**Opportunity:** We can offer LVN/certificate faster

### Sacramento Ultrasound Institute (14.6 mi)
**Location:** Sacramento, CA
**Programs:** Diagnostic Medical Sonography
**Strengths:** Specialty niche, medical imaging
**Weaknesses:** Narrow focus
**Opportunity:** No overlap with our target programs

## Competitive Threats

### Immediate Threats
1. **Campus Sacramento** - Offers MA with Phlebotomy at 9.1 mi
2. **Carrington College** - National brand, accredited, financial aid
3. **Community Colleges** - Free/low cost (Folsom Lake College)

### Emerging Threats
1. **Online programs** - Remote-friendly options for coding
2. **Employer-sponsored training** - Hospitals training internally
3. **New market entrants** - Others see same opportunity

### Mitigation Strategies
1. **Accreditation** - Match competitor credentials
2. **Local focus** - Externships in Folsom/Roseville employers
3. **Price competitiveness** - Beat Campus Sacramento pricing
4. **Speed** - Faster completion than community college
5. **Job placement** - Guarantee interviews with partners

- Population 25-54 interested in healthcare careers: ~15,000

- Estimated healthcare program graduates/year: 500-700
- Market appears undersupplied based on job postings

### Our Target
- Year 1: 50-80 students (capture 10-15% of market)

- Year 3: 250-300 students (with accreditation and Title IV)
