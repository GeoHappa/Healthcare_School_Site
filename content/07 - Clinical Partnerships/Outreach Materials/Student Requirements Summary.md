## Health & Safety Requirements

### Immunizations

| Vaccine | Requirement | Verification |
|---------|-------------|--------------|
| **Hepatitis B** | 3-dose series complete OR positive titer OR signed declination | Documentation from provider |
| **MMR** | 2 doses OR positive titers (all 3 components) | Immunization record or lab results |
| **Varicella** | 2 doses OR positive titer OR documented history of disease | Immunization record or lab results |
| **Tdap** | 1 dose within last 10 years | Immunization record |
| **Influenza** | Current season (required Sept-April) | Immunization record |

### Tuberculosis Screening

| Test | Requirement |
|------|-------------|
| **TB Skin Test (PPD)** | Negative, within 12 months of placement |
| **OR QuantiFERON Blood Test** | Negative, within 12 months |
| **If Positive History** | Chest X-ray (normal) + physician clearance letter |

### CPR/BLS Certification

| Requirement | Accepted Providers |
|-------------|-------------------|
| **BLS for Healthcare Providers** | American Heart Association |
| | American Red Cross |
| **Must be current** | Valid through externship completion |

## Background & Screening

### Criminal Background Check

| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Type** | National criminal background check |
| **Provider** | [Approved vendor name] |
| **Timeframe** | Completed within 12 months |
| **Result** | Cleared (reviewed case-by-case if issues) |

**Checked databases include:**
- National criminal database
- Sex offender registry
- Healthcare sanctions/exclusions (OIG, SAM)
- State criminal records

### Drug Screening

| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Type** | 10-panel urine drug screen |
| **Result Required** | Negative |
| **Timeframe** | Within 30 days of placement |

**Substances tested:**
Amphetamines, Barbiturates, Benzodiazepines, Cocaine, Marijuana, Methadone, Methaqualone, Opiates, Phencyclidine (PCP), Propoxyphene

## Supervision Requirements

### School Responsibilities

- Assign Clinical Coordinator as point of contact
- Conduct site visits during externship
- Available by phone for questions/issues
- Provide evaluation forms and competency checklists
- Address any concerns promptly

### Site Responsibilities

- Designate preceptor/supervisor for student
- Ensure student performs only trained procedures
- Provide feedback on student performance
- Complete evaluation at end of externship
- Report any concerns to school immediately
