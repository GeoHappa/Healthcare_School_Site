## Outreach Packet Assembly

### For Mailed/Delivered Packets

Include in this order:
1. Introduction Letter (on letterhead)
2. Program Overview One-Pager
3. Externship Site Benefits
4. Student Requirements Summary
5. Business card

**Presentation:**
- Use a professional folder or envelope
- Print on quality paper
- Include handwritten note if delivering in person

### For Email Outreach

Attach as PDFs:
- Program Overview One-Pager
- Externship Site Benefits

*Send Introduction Letter as email body, not attachment*

## Customization Checklist

Before using these materials, update:

- [ ] School name throughout
- [ ] Address and contact information
- [ ] Program hours and durations
- [ ] Specific tuition/cost information (if included)
- [ ] School logo added
- [ ] Professional design applied
- [ ] Printed on quality paper

---

## Design Recommendations

### Professional Design

Consider using:
- Canva (free templates)
- Adobe InDesign
- Microsoft Publisher

### Branding Elements

- School logo
- Consistent color scheme
- Professional fonts
- Quality imagery

### Print Specifications

- Paper: 80lb text or heavier
- Color: Full color recommended
- Size: Letter (8.5x11)

## Outreach Timeline

| Week | Activity |
|------|----------|
| 1 | Prepare all materials, customize with school info |
| 2-3 | Tier 1 site outreach (independent practices) |
| 4-5 | Follow-up calls, schedule meetings |
| 6-8 | Site visits, begin agreement discussions |
| 8-10 | Finalize first agreements |
| Ongoing | Continue outreach until minimum sites secured |
