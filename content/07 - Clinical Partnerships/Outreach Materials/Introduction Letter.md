# Customization Notes

## For Different Site Types:

### Urgent Care / Primary Care (MA focus)
Add: "Our Medical Assistant students are trained in vital signs, injections, EKGs, specimen collection, and front office procedures—skills directly applicable to your fast-paced clinical environment."

### Laboratories / Blood Banks (Phlebotomy focus)
Add: "Our Phlebotomy students complete extensive classroom training in venipuncture techniques before clinical placement, and are required to complete 50 successful venipunctures and 10 skin punctures under supervision—providing valuable support for your collection team."

### Cardiology Practices (EKG focus)
Add: "Our EKG Technician students are trained in 12-lead electrode placement, rhythm recognition, and artifact troubleshooting—skills that complement your cardiology services."

### Hospitals
Add: "We understand the complexities of hospital operations and student placement. We are prepared to work through your clinical education department and comply with all facility requirements."
