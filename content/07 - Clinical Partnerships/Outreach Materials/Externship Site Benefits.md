## Benefits to Your Practice

### 1. Build Your Hiring Pipeline

| Without Student Program | With Student Program |
|------------------------|---------------------|
| Post job ads, wait for applicants | Train students to YOUR standards |
| Interview unknown candidates | Evaluate performance over weeks |
| Hope skills match the resume | Know exactly what they can do |
| Lengthy onboarding | Minimal training needed |

**Result:** First access to job-ready graduates who already know your systems

---

### 2. Extra Hands at No Cost

- Students are **unpaid** during externship
- No payroll, benefits, or HR burden
- Covered by **school's liability insurance**
- Supervised by your existing staff

**Students can assist with:**
- Patient rooming & vital signs
- Specimen collection & processing
- EKG procedures
- Administrative tasks
- Inventory & stocking

---

### 3. Staff Development Opportunity

Teaching reinforces skills and knowledge. Your staff members who precept students:
- Sharpen their own clinical skills
- Develop leadership abilities
- Gain teaching experience
- Feel valued as mentors

---

### 4. Give Back to the Community

- Help train the next generation
- Strengthen local healthcare workforce
- Build your practice's reputation
- Support Folsom-area residents

---

### 5. Fresh Perspectives

Students bring:
- Current training on latest practices
- Enthusiasm and eagerness to learn
- New ideas and questions
- Energy to your team

## Student Qualifications

**Every student arrives with:**

✓ **Criminal Background Check** - Cleared
✓ **Drug Screening** - Negative
✓ **TB Test** - Negative (within 12 months)
✓ **Immunizations** - Hep B, MMR, Varicella, Tdap, Flu
✓ **CPR/BLS Certification** - Current
✓ **HIPAA Training** - Completed
✓ **Classroom Training** - Theory mastered before externship
✓ **Professional Liability Insurance** - Active
