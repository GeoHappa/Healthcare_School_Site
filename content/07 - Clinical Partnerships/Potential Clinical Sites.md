## Site Visit Checklist

When visiting potential sites:
- [ ] Introduce school and programs
- [ ] Explain student requirements and supervision
- [ ] Discuss scheduling flexibility
- [ ] Inquire about their experience with students
- [ ] Ask about patient volume (for phlebotomy sites)
- [ ] Discuss EHR systems used
- [ ] Get decision-maker contact information
- [ ] Leave materials and business card
- [ ] Follow up within 1 week

## Notes

**Key Insights:**
1. **Phlebotomy sites are abundant** - Vitalant, Quest, Labcorp provide high-volume experience
2. **Independent practices more accessible** - Large health systems have formal processes
3. **Urgent care = ideal MA sites** - High variety of procedures, fast-paced
4. **Cardiology practices needed for EKG** - Folsom Cardiology and Sacramento Heart are priorities
5. **Start relationship-building before BPPE approval** - Get commitments in principle

**Market Advantages:**
- No competing healthcare schools within 30 miles
- Growing population creating demand for trained staff
- New UC Davis clinic (2025) expanding healthcare infrastructure
