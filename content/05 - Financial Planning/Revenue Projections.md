## Key Revenue Assumptions

### Enrollment Assumptions
- 70% of inquiries convert to enrollment
- 10% attrition (drops before completion)
- Average class fill rate: 85%

### Pricing Assumptions
- Tuition increases 3% annually starting Year 3
- Books/supplies fee covers actual costs + 20% margin
- No significant price competition forces discounting

### Market Assumptions
- Folsom market remains underserved
- Healthcare job demand continues strong
- No major competitor enters market

---

## Revenue Growth Drivers

1. **Accreditation (Year 2-3)**
   - Enables CMA certification
   - Stronger marketing claims
   - +15-20% enrollment boost

2. **Program Expansion**
   - Add Dental Assistant (Year 2)
   - Add LVN program (Year 3+)
   - Continuing education offerings

3. **Partnership Revenue**
   - Corporate training contracts
   - Hospital workforce development
   - WIOA/workforce agency referrals

4. **Title IV Financial Aid (Year 3+)**
   - Requires institutional accreditation (ACCSC)
   - Opens enrollment to students who need aid
   - Potential 30-50% enrollment increase
