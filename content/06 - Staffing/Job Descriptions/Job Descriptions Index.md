## Interview Process

### Recommended Steps

1. **Resume Screen**
   - Verify required credentials listed
   - Confirm minimum experience
   - Look for teaching experience (preferred)

2. **Phone Screen (15-20 min)**
   - Verify credentials are current
   - Confirm experience details
   - Assess communication skills
   - Explain position and salary

3. **In-Person Interview (60-90 min)**
   - Panel interview (owner + PD if hired)
   - Review detailed experience
   - Ask scenario-based questions
   - Practical demonstration (see job descriptions)

4. **Skills Assessment**
   - Program-specific competency test
   - Coding test for coding instructor
   - Rhythm interpretation for EKG
   - Venipuncture demo for phlebotomy

5. **Reference Check**
   - Verify employment and credentials
   - Ask about teaching ability
   - Confirm no disciplinary issues

6. **Background Check**
   - Criminal background check
   - License/credential verification
   - Live Scan (if required for BPPE)
