# Admissions Coordinator

---

## Position Overview

**Title:** Admissions Coordinator / Enrollment Specialist
**Department:** Admissions
**Reports To:** Program Director
**Status:** Full-Time, Non-Exempt
**Salary Range:** $40,000 - $55,000 annually ($19-$26/hour)

---

## Position Summary

The Admissions Coordinator is responsible for managing the student enrollment process from initial inquiry through program start. This position serves as the primary point of contact for prospective students, conducts campus tours, explains program offerings, and ensures compliance with BPPE enrollment requirements. The Admissions Coordinator plays a critical role in achieving enrollment targets while maintaining ethical recruitment practices.

---

## BPPE Compliance Requirements

Per California Education Code and BPPE regulations, the Admissions Coordinator must:

### Training Requirements
| Requirement | Timeframe | Documentation |
|-------------|-----------|---------------|
| BPPE enrollment agreement training | Before enrolling students | Training certificate |
| STRF disclosure training | Before enrolling students | Training log |
| Consumer protection regulations | Before enrolling students | Acknowledgment form |
| Annual compliance refresher | Annually | Training record |

### Prohibited Practices
The Admissions Coordinator must NOT:
- Make false claims about employment outcomes
- Guarantee job placement
- Misrepresent program costs or duration
- Pressure students into enrolling
- Offer unauthorized financial incentives
- Enroll students who don't meet admissions requirements
- Backdate enrollment agreements

---

## Essential Duties and Responsibilities

### Enrollment Management (50%)
- Respond to inquiries within 24 hours (phone, email, web leads)
- Conduct campus tours for prospective students
- Explain program offerings, costs, schedules, and career outcomes
- Review admissions requirements with applicants
- Process enrollment applications
- Verify admissions documents (transcripts, ID, prerequisites)
- Complete enrollment agreements per BPPE requirements
- Collect required disclosures and signatures
- Process registration fees and payment plans
- Coordinate with finance for tuition collection

### Student Services (20%)
- Conduct student orientations
- Assist with student intake paperwork
- Track student attendance during first week
- Follow up on no-shows and at-risk enrollments
- Coordinate with instructors on student readiness
- Maintain student files per BPPE requirements

### Marketing Support (15%)
- Participate in community outreach events
- Represent school at job fairs and career events
- Coordinate open house events
- Track lead sources and conversion metrics
- Provide feedback on marketing effectiveness
- Maintain relationships with referral sources

### Compliance & Reporting (15%)
- Ensure all enrollment documents are complete
- Verify STRF disclosure compliance
- Track enrollment statistics
- Generate enrollment reports
- Maintain prospect and student database
- Support BPPE annual report preparation

## Interview Questions

### Experience & Skills
1. "Describe your experience in a sales or enrollment role. What was your typical conversion rate?"
2. "How do you balance meeting enrollment goals with ensuring students are a good fit?"
3. "Tell me about a time you had to explain complex information to someone unfamiliar with the topic."
4. "How do you prioritize when you have multiple prospective students at different stages?"

### Compliance & Ethics
5. "What would you do if a prospective student asked you to guarantee they'll get a job after graduation?"
6. "How would you handle a situation where someone doesn't meet admissions requirements but really wants to enroll?"
7. "Describe your understanding of ethical enrollment practices."

### Scenario Questions
8. "A prospective student is excited about enrolling but expresses concern about the cost. How do you proceed?"
9. "You have a tour scheduled but the student is 30 minutes late. What do you do?"
10. "A current student's family member calls asking about their attendance. How do you respond?"
