# Program Director

---

## Position Overview

**Title:** Program Director / Chief Academic Officer
**Department:** Administration
**Reports To:** Owner/CEO
**Status:** Full-Time, Exempt
**Salary Range:** $65,000 - $85,000 annually

---

## Position Summary

The Program Director is responsible for the overall academic leadership and operational management of [SCHOOL NAME]. This position oversees curriculum development, faculty supervision, student services, and regulatory compliance. The Program Director serves as the primary academic contact with the Bureau for Private Postsecondary Education (BPPE) and ensures all programs meet state regulatory requirements.

---

## BPPE Minimum Qualifications

Per California Education Code and BPPE regulations, the Program Director must meet the following minimum requirements:

### Education Requirements
| Requirement | Minimum | Preferred |
|-------------|---------|-----------|
| Degree Level | Bachelor's degree | Master's degree or higher |
| Field of Study | Healthcare, Education, or Business | Healthcare Administration or Education |
| Accreditation | Regionally accredited institution | |

### Experience Requirements
| Requirement | Minimum | Preferred |
|-------------|---------|-----------|
| Healthcare/Education Experience | 3 years | 5+ years |
| Administrative/Management | 2 years | 3+ years |
| Vocational Education | Preferred | 2+ years |
| Regulatory Compliance | Preferred | BPPE experience |

### Knowledge Requirements
- Understanding of California Education Code as it relates to private postsecondary education
- Knowledge of BPPE regulations and compliance requirements
- Curriculum development and instructional design
- Student assessment and evaluation methods
- Adult learning principles

---

## Essential Duties and Responsibilities

### Academic Leadership (40%)
- Oversee development, implementation, and continuous improvement of all educational programs
- Ensure curriculum meets industry standards and prepares students for certification exams
- Review and approve course materials, textbooks, and learning resources
- Establish and maintain academic policies and standards
- Monitor student academic progress and outcomes
- Coordinate externship placements with clinical sites

### Faculty Supervision (20%)
- Recruit, hire, and evaluate instructors
- Ensure faculty meet BPPE qualification requirements
- Conduct faculty orientation and ongoing professional development
- Observe classroom instruction and provide feedback
- Approve faculty schedules and assignments
- Maintain faculty personnel files with required documentation

### Regulatory Compliance (20%)
- Serve as primary contact with BPPE for all academic matters
- Ensure compliance with all BPPE regulations and reporting requirements
- Prepare and submit annual reports, catalog updates, and program modifications
- Maintain accurate student records in accordance with regulations
- Coordinate CDPH compliance for phlebotomy program
- Prepare for and facilitate regulatory site visits

### Student Services (15%)
- Oversee student orientation and onboarding
- Address student grievances and academic appeals
- Monitor student retention and implement intervention strategies
- Ensure accurate student disclosures and enrollment documentation
- Supervise student services staff

### Operations (5%)
- Participate in budget planning for academic operations
- Coordinate facility and equipment needs for instruction
- Represent the school at industry and community events
- Contribute to strategic planning and institutional development

## Documents Required for BPPE Application

The following documents must be submitted to BPPE as part of the school approval application:

- [ ] **Resume/CV** - Detailed work history showing relevant experience
- [ ] **Official Transcripts** - From all degree-granting institutions
- [ ] **Copies of Licenses/Certifications** - All professional credentials
- [ ] **Background Check Authorization** - For Live Scan fingerprinting
- [ ] **Employment Agreement** - Signed contract with school

## Sample Interview Questions

### Experience & Background
1. Describe your experience in healthcare education administration.
2. What experience do you have with regulatory compliance (BPPE, accreditation)?
3. How have you developed or revised curriculum in previous roles?

### Leadership & Management
4. How do you approach faculty development and evaluation?
5. Describe a time you had to address a significant student complaint.
6. How do you balance compliance requirements with operational needs?

### Knowledge & Skills
7. What do you know about California's BPPE and its requirements for vocational schools?
8. How would you ensure our phlebotomy program meets CDPH requirements?
9. What metrics would you use to measure program success?

### Situational
10. A student files a grievance about an instructor's grading. How do you handle it?
11. BPPE requests updated documentation within 30 days. How do you prioritize?
12. You notice declining student completion rates. What steps do you take?
