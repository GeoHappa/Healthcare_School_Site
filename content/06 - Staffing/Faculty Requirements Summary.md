# Faculty Requirements Summary

---

## Overview

This document summarizes all faculty qualification requirements for BPPE approval. Use this as a quick reference when recruiting and verifying instructor credentials.

---

## BPPE General Requirements

Per California Education Code Section 94885 and BPPE regulations:

> *"An institution shall employ instructors who possess the academic, experiential, and professional qualifications to teach, including a minimum of three years of experience, education, and training in current practices of the subject area they are teaching."*

### Key Principles
1. Instructors must be **qualified in the subjects they teach**
2. Minimum **3 years of relevant experience** required
3. **Current credentials** must be maintained
4. Qualifications must be **documented** for BPPE

## Program Director Requirements

| Requirement | Minimum | Preferred |
|-------------|---------|-----------|
| **Education** | Bachelor's degree | Master's degree |
| **Field** | Healthcare, Education, or Business | Healthcare Administration |
| **Experience** | 3 years healthcare/education | 5+ years |
| **Admin Experience** | 2 years | 3+ years |
| **BPPE Knowledge** | Required | Prior experience |

**Critical:** Must be hired BEFORE BPPE application submission.

## Instructor Requirements by Program

### Medical Assistant Instructor

**Option A: Certified Medical Assistant**
| Credential | Experience | Education |
|------------|------------|-----------|
| RMA (AMT), CMA (AAMA), NCMA (NCCT), or CCMA (NHA) | 3 years as MA | High school minimum |

**Option B: Licensed Nurse**
| Credential | Experience | Education |
|------------|------------|-----------|
| CA RN or LVN license | 3 years clinical | As required for licensure |

**Additional Requirements:**
- Current CPR/BLS (Healthcare Provider)
- No disciplinary actions on credentials
- TB clearance

---

### Phlebotomy Instructor

**IMPORTANT:** Must meet BOTH BPPE and CDPH requirements.

| Credential | Experience | Education |
|------------|------------|-----------|
| California CPT I or CPT II | 3 years phlebotomy | High school minimum |

**Preferred:**
- National certification (PBT, CPT-NHA)
- Prior training/supervisory experience
- 1,000+ venipunctures performed

**Additional Requirements:**
- Current CPR/BLS
- TB clearance
- Knowledge of CDPH program requirements

---

### Medical Coding Instructor

| Credential | Experience | Education |
|------------|------------|-----------|
| CPC (AAPC) or CCS (AHIMA) | 3 years medical coding | High school minimum |

**Alternative Credentials:**
- CCA (AHIMA) + additional coding credential
- CCS-P (AHIMA) + experience

**Additional Requirements:**
- Maintain certification through CEUs
- Knowledge of annual ICD-10/CPT updates

---

### EKG Technician Instructor

**Option A: Certified EKG Technician**
| Credential | Experience | Education |
|------------|------------|-----------|
| CET (NHA), CCT (NHA), or equivalent | 3 years EKG experience | High school minimum |

**Option B: Licensed Healthcare Professional**
| Credential | Experience | Education |
|------------|------------|-----------|
| CA RN, LVN, or Paramedic | 3 years cardiac/EKG experience | As required for licensure |

**Additional Requirements:**
- Current CPR/BLS
- Demonstrated rhythm interpretation competency

## Documentation Checklist

For each instructor, collect and verify:

### Credential Documentation
- [ ] **Certification card** (copy, front and back)
- [ ] **Credential verification** (online lookup or letter from issuing body)
- [ ] **Expiration date** noted
- [ ] **CEU records** (showing maintenance of credential)

### Experience Documentation
- [ ] **Resume/CV** showing 3+ years relevant experience
- [ ] **Experience verification letters** from employers
- [ ] **Reference contacts** (supervisors who can verify experience)

### Education Documentation
- [ ] **Official transcripts** (if applicable)
- [ ] **Degree verification** (for Program Director)

### Additional Documentation
- [ ] **CPR/BLS card** (current Healthcare Provider level)
- [ ] **TB clearance** (within past year)
- [ ] **Background check** authorization and results
- [ ] **Employment agreement** signed

## Multi-Program Instructors

An instructor qualified in multiple areas can teach multiple programs:

### Scenario: MA Instructor with Additional Credentials

| Primary | Plus | Can Teach |
|---------|------|-----------|
| CMA | CPT I | MA + Phlebotomy |
| CMA | CET | MA + EKG |
| CMA | CPT I + CET | MA + Phlebotomy + EKG |

### Scenario: RN Instructor

| If RN Has | Can Teach |
|-----------|-----------|
| 3+ years MA experience | Medical Assistant |
| 3+ years phlebotomy experience | Phlebotomy (with CPT) |
| 3+ years cardiology experience | EKG Technician |

## Compliance Reminders

### Before Each Program Starts
- [ ] Verify all instructor credentials are current
- [ ] Confirm CPR/BLS not expired
- [ ] Update faculty files

### Annually
- [ ] Review credential expiration dates
- [ ] Collect updated CEU documentation
- [ ] Update catalog faculty list
- [ ] File updated documentation

### For BPPE Annual Report
- [ ] Current faculty roster
- [ ] Qualification documentation for any new instructors
- [ ] Any changes to faculty

---

## Red Flags to Avoid

| Issue | Risk | Solution |
|-------|------|----------|
| Expired certification | BPPE non-compliance | Track renewal dates, require 30-day advance notice |
| Unverified experience | Application rejection | Get written verification from employers |
| Missing transcripts | Application delay | Request official copies early |
| Credential for wrong subject | Cannot teach | Match credential to specific program |
| Disciplinary action on license | May disqualify | Check license status before hiring |
